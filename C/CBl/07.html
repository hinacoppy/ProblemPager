Money Play. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=83 White=78
As others have noted this is simply a matter of knowing how many spare pips for Red before his board crashes constitutes a double and how many a take for White.

If Red has 8 spare pips he can redouble (just) and White can take until Red has 19 spare pips at which point it becomes a pass. This position with 15 spare pips is therefore double/take.
