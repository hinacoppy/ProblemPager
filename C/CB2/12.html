A deceptive position: apparently with a sound structure and leading in the race White needs to look more deeply. In fact his forces are split in two and he will find it very difficult to win from here. His home board is a mess and he'll be lucky to put Humpty together again.

At money White can just about scrape a take but personally I'd let this one go. At this score it is a cast-iron drop. There is a high gammon threat and letting your opponent get to Crawford when you have an odd number of points is never a good idea. Take this and you are in double blunder country.
