When I initially analysed this there was quite a difference between the two top plays but a rollout reduced that to 0.007.

In other words, there are two perfectly acceptable game plans. 17/14, 13/4*, is the more aggressive play while 21/18, 17/8, minimises the blots. As you would expect the first play both wins and loses more gammons and it is the winner by the narrowest of margins.

Against a weak opponent I would welcome the complications but might play more conservatively against a strong player.
