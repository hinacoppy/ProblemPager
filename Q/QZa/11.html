Black should take advantage of his stronger board and hit, thereby reducing White’s numbers that cover her 5pt from 24 to 14 and increasing her fans from 0 to 4.
White can hit and cover her 5pt with only 3 numbers, 11 and 31.
Hit-and-down is better than hit-andsplit because it unstacks the midpoint and brings a ninth checker into the zone, giving Black’s attack a better chance of success.
