pips Red=141 White=142

Red wants to clear the bar-point anchor but because the race is close he also wants to block White’s checkers on his 4-pt. Which is more important?

Because the race is close 13/10(3) takes priority. After that the final 3 should be played 6/3. This starts a new point but also gives Red an extra outfield checker to play with while he awaits another double to clear from White’s bar-point.

Second place is a tie between 18/15(2), 8/5, 6/3 and 13/10(3), 8/5. Both of these moves are errors (just). Even in such a simple position there is still much to think about.
