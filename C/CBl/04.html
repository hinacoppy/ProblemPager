Money Play. How should red play 55?
pips Red=147 White=152
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
It is easy to overplay this position by hitting loose on the one-point with 13/3, 8/3, 6/1* but White is favourite to enter and Red will have no direct covers.

Much better is 23/13, 8/3(2) when Red will be 25 pips ahead, with a stronger home board and with a number of threats. On many sequences he will be doubling next turn.

The hitting play is great when it works but the quiet waiting move is far stronger. The hitting play is a double blunder.
