The real choice here is between hitting with 24/16* and prime building with 13/11(2), 6/4(2).

Note this latter play is better than 13/9, 6/4(2) because the 9-pt and the 3-pt do not work well together as they are six pips apart and cannot be part of the same prime. Note also that making the bar with 13/7, 9/7 is the wrong idea. Red must use two of the twos to make his 4-pt if he is making a new point on his side of the board.
