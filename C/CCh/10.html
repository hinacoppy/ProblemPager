If the spare checker on White’s 3-pt was on his ace-point then Red should leave nothing and play 7/4, 7/1.
That slight weakness in White’s position allows Red to play 13/4 although 7/4, 7/1 would not be an error. There is a fine balance in such positions between risk and reward.
