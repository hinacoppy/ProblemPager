Money play. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=108 White=100
A classic middle game double: There is volatility and gammons to be won for Red but White is still very much in the game. A fan by Red and a six by White puts a new complexion on the game.

Red should double – it is nearly a double blunder not to do so. White has an easy take.

If one of White spare checkers on his 3-pt was on the 4-pt the position would not be a redouble – backgammon is indeed a game of fine margins.
