Match Play. 1-1 to 5. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=159 White=42
A ludicrous redouble at money is perfectly correct at this match score. If Red loses he will very likely lose a gammon and the match.

Of course, Red could regret the redouble if he goes on to lose merely a single game. To get an accurate answer you need to estimate the probable wins and losses and gammon losses for each player and then use those percentages with a match equity table to calculate the match-winning chances in all variations.

That is beyond a simple 'Position of the Day' article but suffice it to say that when all the work is done Redouble/Take are the correct actions.

Over the board I have learnt to redouble positions like this at this match score. I am more often right than wrong.
