Money Game. How should Red play 33?
pips Red=113 White=109
I was the team captain with two team members when this one turned up and needless to say a heated debate arose.

Two of us wanted to go with 7/4(2), 8/2* and the other player wanted 8/2(2)*. The majority prevailed, White danced and we won a gammon. But were we right?

Not according to XG and naturally the best move was the one we didn't discuss: 18/15, 8/2*, 5/2. Apparently not leaving the direct shot is the way to go and in fact 8/2(2)* has virtually the same equity as the best play while the play we chose was an error.

I guess the lesson is to think long and hard before leaving a voluntary shot when there are alternatives. Notice that after 18/15, 8/2*, 5/2 if White does hit other than with 11 there will be a plethora of return shots.
