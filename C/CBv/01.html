2019 - Position 100

Match Play. Red leads 2-0 to 5. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=132 White=147
Red is ahead in Position, Race and Threat and so for money this would be double/drop. White loses too many gammons to be able to afford to take.

At this score however, a gammon gives Red overage and, were White to turn the game around and get a recube in, then four points would get him to Crawford.

These factors combine to give White a comfortable take.
