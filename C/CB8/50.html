Money Play. How should Red play 43?
pips Red=56 White=73
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
The gut reaction is to play safe with 5/2, 5/1 but unless White rolls a six and has to move from Red’s 6-pt Red will have the same problem next turn.

If he plays 15/8 he leaves 12 shots instead of 5 but the game becomes a one-roll proposition – a good idea if you are the favourite. Note it also duplicates White’s 2’s. It turns out that this is the right play and 5/2, 5/1 is an error of 0.05.

If you view the position as a ‘Pay Me Now Pay Me lLater?’ problem you get to the right answer.
