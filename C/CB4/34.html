Backgammon and its infinite variety!

I think over the board most would play 8/6, 5/2*. However the quiz factor makes the position easier than it is in real life and once you see 6/4, 5/2* the more you like it.

It increases Red's backgammons by about 4% and at this score that outweighs the relatively minor risks because with a backgammon Red wins the match. Red wins about 84% of time more or less whatever he does. In a money game with the cube on White's side the double slot is an overplay but hitting is still obviously correct.
