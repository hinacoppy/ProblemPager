However, by a tiny margin, XG prefers 5/3, 5/2. Interestingly after 21/16, White should double and Red should take. After 5/3, 5/2 White should not double because the position is not quite volatile enough.

So, would you rather own the cube in this position or not? Not an easy choice. Will your opponent make the correct doubling decisions? In summary, this position is not as easy as it looks. I think it warrants further study.
