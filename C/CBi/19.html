Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=154 White=157
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
This one fools a lot of people. Superficially it looks like a position after White opens 6x and then Red rolls 55 and then White fans.

The difference here is the race as it is virtually equal before Red rolls. In the standard scenario Red is ahead on the pip count by about 15 pips and that accounts for quite a few wins when his blitz stalls.

That is not the case here and White is much better off because of the equal race. This is obviously a very clear double but it is also a very easy take.
