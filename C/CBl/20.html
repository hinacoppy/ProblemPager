Money Play. How should Red play 62?
pips Red=152 White=144
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
When on offence, play offence, when on defence play defence.

Red should be concentrating on offence in this position and not giving White counter-attacking options. Any play involving splitting the back checkers is very weak.

13/5 is reasonable but 13/11, 13/7 is clearly best. In many variations Red will be offering a strong double next turn and it puts the checkers precisely where you want them.
