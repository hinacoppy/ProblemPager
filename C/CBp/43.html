The race is close and all of Red's checkers except the rear ones are well-placed. The obvious plan is to run while White has a weak home board and poorly positioned checkers. The 5 should be played 21/16. To hit that checker White would have to give up one of his key points in the outfield. The 1 should then be used to play 6/5.
