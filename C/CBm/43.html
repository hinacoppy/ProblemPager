Money Game. How should Red play 52?
pips Red=136 White=120
A lot of players would intuitively make the 3-pt but in fact making the pure four-point prime is much better and making the 3-pt is an error.

With twelve checkers in the attack zone after 13/11, 13/8 and the thirteenth not far away Red has good priming chances. The priming chances are not quite so good after 8/3, 5/3. Also because White has a four-point home board Red doesn't want to have to hit loose if he can avoid it so the pure approach turns out to be a better game plan.
