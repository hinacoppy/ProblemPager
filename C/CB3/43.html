Match Play. Red leads 4-1 to 7. Should Red double? If doubled should White take?
pips Red=101 White=121
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
Many players fear doubling when leading because the cube might come back to them on 4 but winning match play is all about effective and accurate doubling decisions.

This would be a very clear double for money and it is also a clear double at this score. Getting to the Crawford Game with your opponent on an odd number of points is very strong.

White has enough racing and hitting chances to win the game but he will be hindered by the lack of his 5-pt which necessarily reduces his winning chances.
