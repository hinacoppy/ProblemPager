Unlimited Games. Should Red Double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=134 White=140
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
If White’s rear checkers were on Red’s ace-point, then this would be double and pass. When the checkers are on Red’s 2-pt it is a very different ball game.
Now this only just a double by a tiny amount and a very trivial take. It is much more difficult to prime White and the 2-pt generates shots earlier than the ace-point game. Consequently, White loses fewer gammons.
So, borderline double, very easy take. Dropping is worse than a double blunder.
