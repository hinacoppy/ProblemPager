The gut reaction is to quickly play 18/15*/13 and many would do so over the board. However, Red does better to play 18/15*, 13/11.

This gives a much better distribution of builders and the blots protect themselves because nearly all of White's points are stripped and barring a double, he will leave a myriad of shots if he return hits himself.

18/15*/13 is an error thus showing how much you have to concentrate to get the most out of a position.
