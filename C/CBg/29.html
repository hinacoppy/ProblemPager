Money Play. How should Red play 33?
pips Red=186 White=136
Nice though it would be to build the home board the priority here is an advanced anchor. The correct anchor is the bar-point, holding White’s four checkers on his mid-point. Making the 4-pt anchor is too committal and gives White space to play behind Red – not something you want to do when you trail in the race.

Therefore bar/22, 24/18 is logical and the final 3 should be played 8/5, starting the most important point in the home board.

The key here is getting the priorities correct, anchor and then home board in that order and not the other way around. Bar/22, 24/18, 8/5 also leaves open the possibility of making the 22-pt at a future point in time – should that be appropriate.
