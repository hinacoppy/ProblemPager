It’s easy to play on automatic here and move 13/4 (yes, I did).

However, the bar-point could be useful here to destroy White’s timing and if the blot is hit it can easily be recirculated. The key point is that White won’t hit with most of his ‘hitting’ numbers so the blot is not really exposed to a double shot.

Even 13/7, 4/1 is better than 13/4.
