2017 - Position 192

Match Play. Red trails 1-2 to 7. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=159 White=156
For money, this is right on the borderline and you can choose whether to double or not. A trivial take of course.

At the match score the double becomes mandatory and it is a blunder not to double. There are market-losing sequences and Red’s structure is very sound while White’s is not. White still has an easy take.

It is remarkable how often a one point difference in the score can radically affect the correct cube actions.
