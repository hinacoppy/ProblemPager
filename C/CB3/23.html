Match Play. Red trails 2-5 to 7. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=153 White=148
Two points get White to the match very efficiently so he should certainly consider taking.

On the downside four points get Red to the Crawford Game and maybe that is too risky?

Red is the favourite but is yet to hit the second checker and is not favourite to do so. Meanwhile White has only one checker back and all his checkers in play. Red has only eight static checkers in the attack zone. For money a double would be premature here.

Red does win 20% gammons but even that is not enough to make this a drop for White. Of course, Red should double because of the volatility and the match score. Even at 3-5 the correct actions are double and take. Dropping here is a blunder.
