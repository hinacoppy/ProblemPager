I often see this position misplayed so thought I would include it as a problem.

Well behind in the race Red should just simply play 13/7 with the 6. This keeps his rear checkers well connected and gives the option of going forwards or falling into a back game.

24/18, allowing multiple double hits, is a bad blunder. 8/2 is a do nothing play.

Bar/21, 13/7 is the only play that is not a blunder.
