What normally happens in positions like this (and happened to me in live play) is that Red rolls a 5 and makes the 6-pt and then when White fans Red wins with a redouble.

That sequence happens 425 times out of 1296 which suggests that the position is volatile and Red should be redoubling now. Even if the blot is not covered and has to be safetied, White may still fan on the 4-pt board and even if he enters Red has lots of outfield coverage and plenty of potential for the further hitting of White's blot.

All of this indicates that it is the take that is in question rather than the redouble but of course White gets some hits as well so in fact White has a comfortable take but Red must redouble now. It is quite a big error not to do so
