b) Match Play. Red leads 1-0 to 5. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?

For money this a clear double and a clear take – Red has quite some work to do to bring the position home.

At the match score it is very different. A gammon wins the match exactly for Red and White's redoubles are not necessarily as efficient as in a money game. Now taking is a quadruple blunder – quite a difference!

Beware perfectly efficient cubes in match play!
