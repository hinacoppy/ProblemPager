Money Game. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=141 White=130
This position turned up in a session with one of my students. I was Red and doubled. He pondered a while and took. The take is very clear. If Red fans and White escapes a checker (except with 55) then it is he who is threatening to win a gammon.

On the other hand things can go horribly wrong for White and it is he who can lose a gammon. I deemed the position to be volatile enough to double.
