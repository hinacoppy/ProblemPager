The 4 is clear - 24/20. In the old days I would have played 6/5 giving myself more numbers to get to the 20-pt but XG often prefers to step up as it does here with 24/23.

Even if the blot on White’s 9-pt wasn’t there this is still the correct move! The only reason I can see is the horror 33 if you stay back. Presumably this is enough to make 24/23 correct. The lesson is to look at how your doubles play in such situations – these are often swing rolls as is the case here.
