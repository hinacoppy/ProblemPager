A ludicrous double for money becomes a valid redouble at this score.

If Red loses he quite often loses a gammon and the match, so he should maximise his own match equity by redoubling now. His numbers are nicely diversified with the 1’s and 2’s that don’t enter hitting White’s checker on the bar-point.

Be honest, would you have thought of redoubling this over the board?
