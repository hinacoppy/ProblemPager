Match Play. Double Match Point. How should Red play 65?
pips Red=198 White=145
There is no reason not to try to win by going forward and so the 5 should be played 13/8*.

At DMP it is important to keep the checkers in play and active so 11/5 would not be correct for the 6. The double hit 13/8*/2* is an overplay – Red doesn't have enough checkers in the attack zone. Therefore it is a choice between 24/18 and 21/15 for the 6.

Because Red still trails in the race and because he wants to make outer board points 21/15 is the narrow winner despite it giving up the 21-pt (at least temporarily). Note that the play duplicates White 1’s and 3’s.

Not an easy problem and you have done well if you thought your way logically to the correct answer.
