I've had a couple of similar positions to this recently and there are two key factors: the number of shots left and the number of checkers off. Future jeopardy is less of an issue.

Here, whichever play Red chooses he will leave 11 shots and therefore the right play by quite some way is to bear off two checkers with 3/off, 2/off. Any other play is an error.
