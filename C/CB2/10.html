Money Play. How should Red play 64?
pips Red=128 White=100
I played 21/17, 21/15 here without too much hesitation and was surprised when XG didn’t like the play. Despite racing when behind my play looked logical, at least to me, as it provides great outer board coverage.

XG’s top play is 21/15, 6/2* which keeps the blocking points, provides outer board coverage and utilises one of the checkers from the overloaded 6-pt. Notice that after this play some of White’s return hits are not that great (22 and 24).

It would seem that this combination of factors outweighs the simple outfield coverage model. Second best is 8/2*, 6/2 which is not quite an error and my play is third and is definitely an error.

This utilisation of checkers continues the theme developed in the weekend article just published. XG puts a high priority on this aspect of the game and therefore so should we.
