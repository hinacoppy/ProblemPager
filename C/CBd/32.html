Money Play. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=139 White=59
Normally a double shot from a back game is not enough to justify a redouble but there is a key difference here - if Red misses the double shot White wonâ€™t have a redouble for some time because he still has to clear his troublesome 11-pt.

This is the key to the position and so the correct actions are redouble and take. Not redoubling is an error and the take is trivial.

This position occurred in the Battersea chouette. Both sides got the cube action correct. Red then missed three double shots in a row and White won with a well-timed redouble to 8. Such is backgammon!
