Match Play. 0-2 to 5. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=89 White=125
In a money game this would be no redouble/beaver! However, at this score Red has a perfectly valid redouble. A gammon wins the game and match for him and, if things go wrong, he is not giving up much equity as, if he fans, he may well be the one losing a gammon and the match anyway.
Not redoubling is a blunder. White has a trivial take of course but I have seen players drop positions like this as White for fear of losing a gammon and the match.
