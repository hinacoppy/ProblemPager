Match Play. Red leads 4-0 to 7. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=19 White=21
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
Never be afraid to double when ahead especially if winning the game will get you to the Crawford Game.

This position is a perfectly double and take, both for money and at this match score. Admittedly White will redouble earlier than usual at the match score but that should not dissuade you from doubling now.

Not doubling is very nearly a double blunder.
