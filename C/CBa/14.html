Money Play. How should Red play 55?
pips Red=166 White=156
If you didn’t play 6/1(2)* with two of the fives then you are living in a pre-bot era. The tempo taken by that play could well be vital.

Obviously the first five is bar/20 so it’s just a question of the fourth one.

The two back checkers should stay connect and so 20/15 is eliminated. Given White’s reasonable board Red does slightly better to play 11/6 rather than 13/8 but it is quite close.

The key point is that making the 1-pt is mandatory.
