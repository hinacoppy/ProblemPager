Money Game. How should Red play 44?
pips Red=136 White=153
The question is whether to hit the white checker on the 9-pt?

It gains in the race but on the downside it gives White the chance to create a back game rather than just a holding game. In this case the downside outweighs the upside and it is better to leave the checker on the 9-pt alone.

After that it should be clear that correct move is 14/10(2), 11/7(2), creating good points and also good landing points for the checkers on the mid-point.
