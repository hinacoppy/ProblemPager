Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled should White take?
pips Red=117 White=106
This is the follow-up to Position 182.

Red’s position looks stronger than it is. Even after a hit he still has two rear checkers to extract and he is not even favourite to hit. The duplication of sixes is also very important.

It is a huge mistake to double here. It’s not quite a beaver but it is closer than you might think.
