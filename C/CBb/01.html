Match Play. Red trails 3-5 to 7. How Red should play 31?
pips Red=93 White=114
I have 100% record on this position! Nobody I have shown it to gets the correct play.

For money or virtually any other match score Red would just shuffle his checkers in his home board with 6/2, or 6/3, 2/1 being the top picks.

However, at this match score gammons are optimised for Red as they win the match for him exactly. So by now you have probably found 13/12, 6/3!! If not hit the play helps Red to bring home his outfield checkers and, if he is hit and rolls a 4, his gammon chances increase significantly, so much so that any play other than 13/12, 6/3 is an error. Remarkable.

I will add this one to my list of favourite positions!
