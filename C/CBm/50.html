For money, hitting the second checker would clearly be right because of the extra gammons and 23/22*, 13/10 is the best play.

At DMP gammons don’t count so the pure 8/5, 6/5 must come into consideration. This is what I did over the board employing Falafel’s maxim, “make the 5-pt and then think” but I wasn’t sure.

It turns out that (a) 23/22*, 13/10 (b) 23/222*, 11/8 and (c) 8/5, 6/5 are all within 0.007 equity points of each other so I suspect everybody got this one correct!
