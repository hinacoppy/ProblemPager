This one is easy if you remember the prime rule of blitzing: never give your opponent the chance to anchor if you can avoid it. Here that means hitting 6/5* rather than 13/12*.

The second thing to do is not to fight a war on two fronts and so 22/18 is irrelevant. The right four is 14/10 bringing more firepower to the blitz. Any play that doesn't hot on the 5-pt is a blunder. Lesson learnt!
