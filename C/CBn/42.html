Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=112 White=113
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
This is an excellent benchmark position. It is a very clear double as Red could easily lose his market.

White can take or pass – this is right on the borderline.

Every aspect of a position matters. Move the blot on White’s ace-point to his 3-pt and the position becomes a take despite White losing two pips in the race. This is because he can now more easily build a perfect home board so, in the variations where he hits a shot, he will be able to win with an immediate redouble in most instances.
