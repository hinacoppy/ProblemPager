“How to play the 5? Under certain circumstances, a key goal would be to stop White from getting a second anchor. Thus, 6/1* would be indicated. However, here I think it is the wrong plan. White's timing is poor, so stopping the second anchor isn't so crucial, and Red doesn't really have the ammunition to follow up the hit. Red himself has 4 checkers back, so I think the more positional play of Bar/16 is better.”
