Obviously the 2 is played 18/16* but is the 5 played 18/13 or 16/11?

Both plays leave 9 shots. The simple answer is to keep the blots connected which makes it much easier to tidy them up next turn, thus leaving fewer shots next time.

18/16*/11 is very nearly a double blunder!
