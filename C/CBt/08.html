Money Play. Should Red double? If, doubled, should White take?
pips Red=151 White=131
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
A classic middle game position. Black has the advantage due to his excellent structure and command of the outer boards. Meanwhile White has a racing lead and all of checkers are still in play, even if they are somewhat awkwardly placed.

The volatility is such that Red should double now. Not doubling is nearly a double blunder. White has a relatively comfortable take, dropping would be an error but not a blunder.
