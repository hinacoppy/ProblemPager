Match Play. Red trails 5-6 to 11. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=109 White=127
Red’s position look powerful but White has many ways to win especially when Red starts with a couple of non-point making small numbers. Even when thing go badly White will be likely to be able to fall back on a well timed ace-point game.

For money this is a very easy take – the double is very clear due to the volatility of the position. The match score makes the decision more difficult especially when White can see some gammon losses but even so it would be very nearly a blunder to pass.
