If White doesn’t hit on the ace-point Red will have a lot of playability as he can play behind White’s anchor. If one blot is hit the second blot may mean Red has to drop a double.

The rollout isn’t close 16/10 wins hands down. 16/15, 6/1 is midway between an error and a blunder (-0.06).
