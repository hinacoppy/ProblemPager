Money Play. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=10 White=3
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
This will be a known reference position for many of you and if it isn’t it is now!

Over the board I would use Robertie’s Addition Method. A two roll position gives White 14%. Two successive ones by Red give him another 9% and there are combinations (e.g. 32 followed by 32) that should give him the requisite 2% to take.

Not also that after 21 by Red White has a perfectly correct redouble.

In fact White has a comfortable take of Red’s redouble and dropping is very nearly a blunder.
