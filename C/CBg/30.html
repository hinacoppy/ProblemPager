Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=73 White=93
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
This type of position appears in Woolsey’s Encyclopaedia of Backgammon. If the two Red checkers on the 9-pt were on the 10-pt then this would be double/take. With the checkers on the 9-pt the position should be double/pass.

However, Red can’t play 41 safely – does that make a difference? It does, but not enough to make this a take. You would be giving up 0.05 equity points if you took this.
