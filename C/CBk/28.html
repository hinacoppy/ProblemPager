For money this is a big double but a very easy take due to White’s large racing lead.

At this match score gammons are perfectly efficient for Red but he won’t win that many – in fact about 5%. Even so two points are very valuable to Red and he wins about 73% of the time.

The bottom line is that White can take or pass this double – it is right on the borderline, which means that judging the skill differential between the two players will need to be considered in the equation.
