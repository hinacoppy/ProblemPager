“Don’t panic. Mr. Mainwaring!”, to quote Dad’s Army.

First win the game and then win the gammon. 5/4*/1 looks tempting but it is an overplay against a four-and-a-half point board. The simple 15/12, 6/5 is the best play. It leaves only 3 shots (all of which leave return shots) and White’s blot may still be under threat next turn.
