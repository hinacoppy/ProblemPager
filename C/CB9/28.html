Towards the end of a game there is seldom much choice as to what is the best move. That is not so true in the opening where a number of different game plans may be valid.

Over the board I played 8/4*, 6/3* because that us my style but I wasn’t sure about my play. It turned out to be best by 0.001!! The prosaic 16/13, 6/2 is equally OK and even 16/13, 8/4* is OK. Note, however, that 24/21, 8/4* is the wrong idea as it puts the rear checker in too much danger. That move is nearly a double blunder.

So here you can play the move that best suits your style but you should also consider your opponent. If he is uncomfortable in volatile positions and doesn’t handle the cube well go for the double hit.
