Most formulae, including Reichart’s, get this one correct. Clear double and clear take.

What if you don’t like formulae? The old-fashioned way of looking at this would be roughly:

This is a more or less and three-roll positions. I trail by three pips but the roll is worth 4 pips so actually I am marginally ahead. A small lead in a short race is nearly always worth a double so I will double. Equally well my opponent trails by only 1 pip (after the adjustment for the roll) therefore he probably has a take.

So, hard formulae or common-sense analysis – the choice is yours.
