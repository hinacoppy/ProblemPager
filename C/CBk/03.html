This must be a double. The volatility is sky-high and Red will win a fair percentage of gammons (actually about 16%).

However, Red will fan 44% of the time and then White can either hit red’s other blot or cover his 6-pt and hit later. This gives White a high percentage of wins – 40% to be precise.

Red’s equity is only 0.368 – normally not nearly enough but the very high volatility makes this a double. If Red already owned the cube then gammon would already be active and so Red should not redouble this position.
