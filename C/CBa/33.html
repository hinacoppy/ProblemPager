Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=20 White=20
Most players see 41, 42, and 43 and decide to wait a roll.

However, the optimist sees that he can lose his market by the next roll with a lot of sequences and spins the cube. This is a close but perfectly correct double.

If Red already holds the cube then he should not redouble.

This is a good example of the classic difference between doubling and redoubling.
