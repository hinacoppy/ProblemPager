Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?

Obviously, this is a very clear double. The perfect prime makes Red a big favourite and if he picks up another checker or two then a gammon is a possibility.

The question is can White counter-prime Red and force him to break that prime? The answer is yes he can but does it happen often enough to give him a take? That possibility plus the chance of winning from the 3-pt holding game puts this right on the cusp of take/pass.
