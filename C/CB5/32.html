Match Play. Red trails 0-1 to 7. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=136 White=92
Red is the favourite here and any 5 will lose his market but in a money game his gammon losses make this no redouble.

At 0-0 to 7 this is borderline redouble as a gammon wins the match for either player. Once you trail in a match the redouble becomes more powerful because if White wins a gammon he will have overage (too many points). At this 0-1 score it is a double blunder not to redouble!

Ten years ago nobody made redoubles in positions like this but they are becoming more common as we learn more about match play. If you got this one correct then well done.
