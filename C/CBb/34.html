Behind in race, threat and position can White muster a take here?

The only thing going for him is the structure of Red’s home board. I call this a Swiss cheese board – lots of holes. Red also owns both his 8-pt and his 2-pt and they both cannot be part of the same prime.

Surprisingly these tiny flaws are enough to give Red the skinniest of takes though it’s really no bargain. I wouldn’t argue against a chouette partner who wanted to drop this one.
