pips Red=124 White=90
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
Red must hit 8/7*. Then do you play 9/5 or 8/4 with the four?
There is a huge difference between the plays. After 9/5, 8/7* and a fan by White, White must pass Red’s redouble because of the score (trivial take for money). After 8/4, 8/7* and a fan White has a comfortable take of Red’s redouble because there are only two cover numbers for the blot on the 5-pt.
A slightly greater risk earns a huge reward. Remarkably 8/4, 8/7* is a bad blunder.
