Money Play. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=97 White=79
Is Red's outer board sufficient compensation for the 18-pip racing deficit?

Yes, it is, and it would be a bad error not to double this position.

On his side White's home board is a strong as Red's although he does have one dead checker. There are few gammons on either side. White escapes and/or hits often enough to give him a comfortable take.
