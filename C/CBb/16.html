2018 - Position 58

Money Play. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=116 White=91
We learn through repetition and reinforcement so Positions 57 and 58 should be viewed together.

This is not as clear as Position 57 but the facts that Red has 19 hitting numbers and White can’t redouble if Red misses are key. In addition, Red wins a reasonable percentage of gammons. Finally, White’s ugly home board is also a factor as it gives Red additional long-term chances.

On the downside Red may get hit on his 2-pt and he does trail by 25 pips in the race.

Combining all these factors is difficult, especially over the board. Hardly anybody would drop this as White so the opponent factor doesn’t really come into it.

XG has this as a double (just). Dropping, however, would be a decablunder!
