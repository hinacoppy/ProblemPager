For money this is a redouble and a drop – too many gammons.

At this score technically Red should treat this as too good/not good enough and play on for the gammon or until the situation becomes clearer. However, the decision is close.

Practically this is a great redouble – you will get a lot of drops and a 4-0 lead in the match.

Don’t be fooled into thinking the position is too good double. Consider a roll of 65 for Red which should be played 16/10*, 9/4* - great except when White rolls a 4 in return. This demonstrates there is much wok still to be done so be grateful if your opponent passes.
