Money Play. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=92 White=86
This is a very unusual position so you won’t have many reference positions to fall back on.

A hit for Red followed by a fan for White will be a redouble/pass but that's sequence doesn’t happen that often. Red can probably count on a few hits along the way as he brings his checkers round but equally well White will also gain ground with hitting – his problem is his virtually non-existent home board.

White obviously has no fear of the gammon. Red needs 23 crossovers to bear-off while White needs 18 – an indication that Red is behind at the moment.

The rollouts have this as a borderline double/no double and a very easy take (more than a double blunder to pass). From a practical perspective I would double this every time despite the lack of volatility – I know I will get a lot of passes.
