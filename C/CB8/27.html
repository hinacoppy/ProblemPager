Money Play. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=108 White=102
Clearly this a redouble due to the volatility of the position and the diversity of Red’s numbers.

What perhaps is not so clear is that White has an easy take. That five-point home board is powerful and as well as Red crashing his board before escaping his rear checker, White can also win by hitting a return shot or anchoring in Red’s board and then winning from there.

Big double, easy take.
