Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=128 White=122
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
There are enough market losers (especially as White doesn’t have an anchor) to make this a double and not doubling is an error.

However, Red has only a two-point home board to White’s four-point board and the race is close so this is a trivial take.

A lot of people wait a roll in positions like this but the volatility makes doubling clearly correct.
