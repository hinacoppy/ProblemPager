The key to this position is the score. For money it would be a small error to double as Red doesnâ€™t have enough checkers in the zone.

The score changes things significantly. A gammon for Red gives him the match with perfect efficiency so that should give him an alert. At this score not doubling is on the edge of being a blunder!

Remember, before you start any game in a match always think about the score and decide what your doubling strategy should be. At this score any sight of a gammon should give you pause for thought as you should double earlier than normal.
