Match Play. Red trails 2-3 to 5. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=108 White=122
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
Red has a lot of work to do here and while there is a gammon threat it is a small one. If he doesn’t hit the White blot he could quickly run into trouble trying to clear his rear checkers.

The volatility is relatively low and although it is close Red should not double this position. For money it would be a double blunder to offer the cube here.

One of the reasons for not doubling here is what I define as “Cube Future Value” (CFV). To understand this concept read the longer weekend article tomorrow.
