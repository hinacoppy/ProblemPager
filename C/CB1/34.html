Money Game. How should Red play 64?
pips Red=75 White=98
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
The generally accepted theory for positions like this is hit on the ace-point unless White has a full prime.

Here though Red has very little spare timing if things go awry. Move Red's spare checker from his 4-pt to his mid-point and hitting is clear. I'll leave you to experiment with moving the spare checkers to determine the break-even point between hitting and playing safe.

In our position 11/5, 6/2 is correct by a small margin.
