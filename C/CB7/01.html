Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=126 White=86
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
I was on the five-man team as Red against the box and his partner. Two players doubled very quickly and two waited. I was certain this wasn’t a double but the box was someone who I thought would misevaluate the position and so I doubled.

I was vindicated when the box convinced his partner to drop all three doubles and play against the other two, so I very effectively stole a point.

In fact this is a huge no double and very nearly a beaver. White’s racing lead plus the strength of his home board give him many ways to win.
