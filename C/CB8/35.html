There are so many ways of playing this 64. Neither of the double hits is correct because any return 4 from White wipes out the advantage.

The second best play is 24/20*/14 which gains in the race and makes the 14-pt but again, any anchoring play from White gives him very good long-term chances.

As is often the case in the opening securing taking a fixed asset is correct and the best play is 10/4, 8/4*.
