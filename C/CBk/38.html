Not 24/13 which is too weak – the checkers need to work.

Slotting the Red bar-point with 16/11, 13/7 is also not the right idea – that is an overplay.

It is a choice between 24/18, 16/11 (with meaningful duplication of threes) and 16/10, 13/8. It is a close call. XG has a very slight preference for 24/18, 16/11 but the two plays are virtually identical.

The key lessons are to avoid the overly cautious 24/13 and not to slot the bar in such positions.
