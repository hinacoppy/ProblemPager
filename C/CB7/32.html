Back from skiing with no damage and it was great week in Val d'Isere. So off we go once more.

At first sight this position looks like a drop. Red is ahead in race, threat and position and that would support the drop theory.

But Red’s board is somewhat of a Swiss cheese – too many holes. Move the two checkers on Red’s 2-pt to his 5-pt and it becomes a massive drop but those holes give White anchoring and back game opportunities. So much so that this is actually a very easy take indeed.

Of course the double is clear as there are numerous market losing sequences.
