Unlimited Games. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=147 White=139
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
A deceptive position where Red looks stronger than he is because of his outer board coverage.

Meanwhile, White has a four-point prime that is set to become a five-point prime and his rear checkers can see daylight.

Because White’s rear checkers are on Red’s ace-point Red has just enough of an advantage to double but it is very close. White, of course, has a trivial take. Dropping would be a massive blunder.
