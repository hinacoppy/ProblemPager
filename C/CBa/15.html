Match Play. Red leads 8-5 to 13. Should Red double, if doubled should White take?
pips Red=142 White=158
For money the threat of making his 5-pt gives Red a perfectly correct double. White has a very easy take as he has counter-play even if Red does make the point.

However, the position is very gammonish for both sides and so at this score Red should leave the cube in the middle and hope to give White a much more difficult choice on his next turn.

A double by Red here would be a triple blunder! Yet again the match score dominates the decision-making.
