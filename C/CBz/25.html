Money Play. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=99 White=83
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
A classic reference position:

Red will nearly certainly lose his market if he rolls a six. Even if he doesn’t White’s home board will deteriorate unless he rolls a 6. A volatile position so the redouble is correct.

From White’s perspective, if Red doesn’t roll a six (25/36) and he does roll a 6 (11/36) he will have a strong position (but not yet a redouble). That scenario alone gives White nearly 25%. There are plenty of other winning variations for White and his gammon losses are less than 5% so he has a comfortable take.
