It doesn’t have to be beautiful if it does the job.

9/4(2), 7/2(2) just looks so wrong and it is. 9/4(2), 6/1(2)* puts White on the bar and creates some space for Red behind White’s anchor as he tries to bear-in his remaining checkers.

Of course, 25% of the time Red gets the bonus of White staying on the bar, making the bear-in just that little bit easier.
