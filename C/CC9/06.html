Unlimited Games. How should Red play 61?
pips Red=149 White=154
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
The double hit, 8/2*/1*, gains very little and just puts a checker out of play.

Similarly Red can’t give up his major asset which is White’s 5-pt by playing 20/13*.

This leaves the simple 23/16 which can do no harm and works well if it isn’t hit. Any other play is a blunder or worse.
