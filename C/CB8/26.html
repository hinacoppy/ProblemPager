Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=119 White=135
Given this as a quiz problem you will think it through properly. Over the board too many would see White’s five-point home board and take a roll. That would be a bad mistake!

White’s three tapped checkers are in real trouble and without a 2 very soon his position will crash. Any 1 by Red will nearly certainly lose his market.

Not doubling here is a triple blunder. Doubling activates Red’s gammons. What about the take? The rollout has it as a dead heat between dropping and passing. If your glass is half-full you can take, if it’s is half-empty you can pass!
