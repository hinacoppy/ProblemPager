My apologies for the diagram error. At 2-2 to 5, which is what the original diagram showed, this would be a redouble and take as owning the cube would be of no value to White and Red is a clear favourite.

At 2-2 to 7 this is (just) not a redouble. White gets enough value from possible redoubles to 8 that Red should hold the cube for a roll. The key is to at least consider redoubling here. If you looked at the pip count and quickly rejected redoubling then you were not thinking through the implications of the match score.
