Match Play. Red trails 3-4 to 7. How should Red play 32?
pips Red=24 White=102
For money the two plays 3/off, 2/off and 3/off, 3/1 are tied in equity.

However, backgammon is a game of small differences and a gammon for Red gives him the match with perfect efficiency. The chance that White will fan with 22 may seem insignificant but it is enough to make the hitting play correct at this match score.

So much so that 3/off, 2/off is a significant error. Never forget the match score!
