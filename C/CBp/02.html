Red is running out of time and his rear checkers must move while his home board is stronger than White's.

Rear checkers should stay connected and so 24/23, 24/18 is slightly better than 24/17, although it is close and 24/17 is not an error. The key to the position is to mobilise the rear checkers ASAP, i.e. now!
