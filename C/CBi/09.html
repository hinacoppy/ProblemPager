Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=151 White=173
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
This is a position where PRaT (Position Race Threat) doesn’t quite work.

Red is ahead in all three and that normally indicates double/drop but this is early in the game
and Red’s threats just aren’t threatening enough to warrant a double. Also, note that Red would be breaking the Dimitri Rule – never double before you have at least one more point in your home board.

This is a big no double and obviously White has a trivial take if doubled.
