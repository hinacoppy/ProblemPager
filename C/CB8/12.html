Money Play. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=139 White=135
This position demonstrates the negative value of a man on the bar. And of course, White has a broken prime!

It may look as if White will have trouble tunnelling out of Red’s board but the same could be true for Red if ends up with four checkers behind White’s broken prime. He may crack before White.

It is more the redouble than the take which is in question here. The take is trivial but actually the double is actually mandatory because a lot of players will give this one up as White.
