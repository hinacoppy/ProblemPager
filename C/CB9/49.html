Money Play. How should Red play 52?
pips Red=96 White=102
This one is not close.

8/6, 8/3 leaves no shots and provides an extra attacker against White’s blot on Red’s ace-point.

13/11, 13/8 (in theory clearing a difficult point) is an error I often see in this type of position. In fact, it is actually a blunder rather than an error. Giving gratuitous shots against a five-point home board is rarely correct.
