Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=115 White=172
A classic middle game position.

Lots of potential for Red and a fair number of market losing sequences. White, on the other hand had a good home board and a lot of defensive possibilities, ranging from holding games to back games or even winning by going forward.

Not surprisingly, this is a big double, but also a very clear take. The incorrect action, in either case, is a blunder.
