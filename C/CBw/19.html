Red has doubled and so has to win the game with his checkers. If he plays safe with9/3, 6/2 what will he do next time?

Once again, the aggressive play is the right one. 13/7, 8/4* is the right move. All the other hitting plays are better than the non-hitting plays.

The key point about hitting is that it increases the number of gammons won by a huge percentage. 9/3, 6/2 wins 4% gammons. 13/7, 8/4* wins 21% gammons. Yes, that play loses more gammons as well but nowhere near enough to compensate for not hitting.
