Money Play. How should Red play 64?
pips Red=163 White=152
8/2, 6/2 was played over the board but that is the wrong idea.

Red needs to promote contact while he has the better home board. 13/9, 13/7 is one approach but if White doesn't hit there is no pressure on him on the other side of the board so he is free to move checkers in his outer boards.

This is similar to an opening 64 but Red has the advantage of his 5-pt so 24/18, 13/9 is even clearer here than it would be as a first move.
