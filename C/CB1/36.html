Money Play. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=26 White=35
Trice’s Stack & Straggler formula works well for this one.

Red’s Effective Pip Count is 43 (using the 7n+1) formula. White’s EPC is 46 (6x3.5 plus 25 for the straggler). White can take if his deficit is no more than the number of rolls to go minus 3. Here that is 7-3=4.

This gives White a bare take but Trice’s formula assumes the straggler is on the board and not on the bar. That difference swings it from a bare take to a bare pass which is what XG says as well. It is a pass by a mere 0.0019.
