pips Red=131 White=130
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
This is a prime versus prime position and so Red should make his full prime with 13/7(2).
The tactical 13/10, 9/3*, 6/3 is an error and 9/3(2) is very nearly a blunder.
Hopefully Red can use the cube to win the game. If White already owned the cube then 13/10, 9/3*, 6/3 would be marginally the better play.
