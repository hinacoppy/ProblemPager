Money Play. How should Red play 54?
pips Red=157 White=150
When 64 is played 8/2, 6/2 in the opening many people don’t know the correct responses. This also applies to first few moves of the game.

This game started with both players moving 8/2, 6/2 and then White rolling 61. It looks intuitive to play 24/15 or 24/20, 13/8 with Red’s 54 but this allows White to employ the tactic that he wants to use after making his 2-pt. That tactic is to attack as the priming option is an unlikely game plan.

Very clear here is 13/9, 13/8 which thwarts White’s plan and develops Red’s position.

Note that if both players had opened with 53 and not 64 then the right move would be 24/20, 13/8. Interesting ne c’est pas?
