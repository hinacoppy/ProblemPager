(b) Money Play. How should Red play 55?


In a money game making two inner board points is clear. White has to drop a double if he fans.

Interestingly at gammon go XG has 13/8(2), 6/1(2)* as clearly the better play, a result I find surprising. It rates 8/3(2), 6/1(2)* as a blunder at gammon go despite the play winning more gammons. Of course, Red will be cubing with far less advantage than normal at this score thus activating the potential for four points through a doubled gammon. I suspect the result comes from much more efficient use of the cube at this particular score.
