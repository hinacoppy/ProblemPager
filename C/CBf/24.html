With three checkers stuck behind a solid four-point prime it is more the double than the take that is in question in this position.

XG has Red winning just enough gammons to make this a double but only by the thinnest of margins so it is certainly not an error to wait here.

The take is trivial of course. If you dropped this one you have a little soul-searching to do!
