2014 - Position 160

Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled should White take?
pips Red=145 White=109
This is a deceptive position. Red's double is very clear. He has twenty-two (don't forget 33 and 55) numbers that hit and give him a big edge. But on the other fifteen (perhaps other than 44) White's position looks good despite the trapped rear checker.

White has a comfortable take and it would be a double blunder to pass. However I am betting many would do so over the board. Even if Red does pick up the second checker if White can anchor that provides him with some additional winning chances.
