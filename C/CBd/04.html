Money Game. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=142 White=94
I dropped this quickly as did everybody else I have showed it to.

However, XG rates this as the skinniest of takes! Once you start to look at the various permutations of how White can win from here the answer becomes a little more credible but it’s still difficult to see White getting to the necessary percentage to make this a take.

But XG has White winning 27% of the time with 12% gammons. One factor is that the cube is very useful to White and he doesn’t always have to escape both rear checkers to win the game.

He can escape one and have one at the edge of Red’s prime and that is enough in some variations. An interesting position I think you will agree.
