Money Play. How should Red play 65?
pips Red=167 White=144
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
White is not threatening much on his side of the board so a defensive play such as 24/18, 23/18 is not warranted.

Hitting with 13/8* is indicated to send the checker to the bar. After that 24/18 is the wrong idea, giving White a good six when on the bar. The correct play is the simple 13/8*, 13/7, developing the spares on the mid-point and virtually equalising the race.
