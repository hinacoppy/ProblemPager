Money Play. How should Red play 62?
pips Red=131 White=146
When I first posted this I thought it would be another case for the broken prime but in fact there are three viable plays.

11/5, 6/4 – keeping the broken prime and slotting the key 5-pt

11/5, 7/5 – making the 5-pt but breaking the prime

20/14, 11/9 – keeping the ‘pure’ broken prime.

The three plays are very close to each other in terms of equity with 11/5, 6/4 winning by 0.001.

Thus proving that more than one game plan is often viable and sometimes there is no ‘best’ move.
