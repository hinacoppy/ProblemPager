Most of the time White will win this by having a well-timed 2-pt game, if he wins at all.

The five is mandatory, 23/18. After that there is very little to choose between 13/12, 6/5 and 2/1. Interestingly XG has 13/12 just ahead of the other plays but there is only a 0.014 difference which is perhaps surprising but of course, gammons don’t matter. 13/12 allows Red to make his bar-pt more easily when those two checkers aren’t hit.
