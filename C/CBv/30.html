Match Play. Red leads 6-1to 7 (Post-Crawford). How should Red play 63?
pips Red=141 White=137
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
The race is close so that implies that hitting will be correct. The alternative is 11/5, 8/5, making the important 5-pt and keeping the anchor in White’s board.

The latter play reduces the percentage of gammons lost but doesn’t compensate for the reduced number of wins. When in doubt, hit and that maxim certainly applies in this position. Red will have to leave the anchor soon so it is best that he does that with the tempo gain of a hit.
