Match Play. Red leads 6-3 to 7. How should Red play 11?
pips Red=66 White=99
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
So should Red play to win with bar/24, 8/5* or play not to lose a gammon with bar/24, 8/7(2), 2/1?

If Red had only a three-point board the hit would be too risky. With a five-point board the hit would be clear. With a four-point it is close but hitting is correct. If Red elects not to hit he will still lose a gammon 11% of the time.

However, by hitting he increases his game winning chances by 15% from 11% to 26%. Not hitting is actually an error.
