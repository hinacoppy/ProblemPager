Money Game. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=151 White=133
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
It is nearly a double blunder not to double here.

Red’s four-point is very strong indeed (“four is more than you think” – Kent Goulding) and he is threatening to extend it. White’s structure is ugly and difficult to improve. Many wouldn’t double because of the racing deficit but this is all about the strength of the position – that deficit will soon be made up.

White still has a comfortable take because of his anchor and the fact that none of his checkers are out of play.
