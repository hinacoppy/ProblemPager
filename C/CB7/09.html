Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=137 White=146
A classical early blitz position with huge volatility.

It is clearly right to double – Red’s market may have disappeared by next turn. However White has a very easy take. Red’s 6’s are duplicated and even if White is hit he may anchor or at least enter immediately.

A triple blunder not to double, only a double blunder if your passed the cube!
