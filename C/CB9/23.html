b) Money Play. White owns a 2 cube. How should Red play 51?


Red must hit with the 1 in both variations. 8/3*/2 would be a quintuple blunder.

AT DMP you should be playing purely and maximising checker use. Gammons don’t matter, Prime vs. Prime theory says step to the edge of your opponent’s prime and hit him away from the edge of your prime. That points to 24/23*, 8/3* and indeed that is correct play by a long way.

For money gammons do count and here Red loses just enough gammons with 24/23*, 8/3* to give 24/23*, 7/2 the nod as the best play, but it is close and 24/23*, 8/3* is not an error.
