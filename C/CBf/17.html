(CBf-17)   2016 - Position 116
Match Play. Red leads 5-2 to 7. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=81 White=113
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
Players are always reluctant to double when it gives the opponent an automatic redouble which is the case here.

However, the art of winning won matches is to know when to offer such doubles. Surprisingly at this score the doubling window is still quite wide and Red has a perfectly correct double and White has a perfectly correct take.

It is actually an error not to double but not a blunder. Of course, this assume both players are of equal strength. If they are not then adjustments must be made but that is beyond the scope of a ‘Position of the Day’ article.
