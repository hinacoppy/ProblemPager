pips Red=88 White=126
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
A nice easy one.

Despite White's five-point board Red must play 9/3*, 8/3. It loses 2% more games but wins 20% more gammons - a fair exchange in anybody's books!

After the safe play Red will still have trouble bearing in and hence only the 2% increase in winning chances.
