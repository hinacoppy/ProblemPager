Match Play. Red trails 1-3 to 5. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=114 White=86
The 2-away vs. 4-away score gives rise to some very interesting cube decisions especially when the trailer has a gammon threat. Here the gammon threat is real at about 11%. Does that make this a double?

The answer is no, but it is close. Move the White blot from his 2-pt to his 5-pt, giving Red 13 shots instead of 11 and it becomes a blunder not to double.

My benchmark for such positions is that I need 13 shots before I will offer a double, as is the case here.
