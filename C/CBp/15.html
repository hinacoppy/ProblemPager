Money Play. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=85 White=61
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
Clearly a huge double for Red with so many market losers.

Can White squeeze out a take given he will eventually miss with ones and fours and even if he enters he still has to escape from red's board? In addition his entering 42 exposes another blot.

The rollouts make this a take by a tiny margin but it's no bargain. Given it is borderline it is a good reference position to add to your library.
