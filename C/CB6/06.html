(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
It's surprising how early one can double positions like this and in fact not doubling here is virtually a blunder. White has a comfortable take.

White's structure is not ideal by any means and that blot on the ace-point is key. Give him his ace-point by moving the spare checker from his 2-pt and it becomes no double/take.

As I have stressed before take care to study every element of a position before coming to a decision.
