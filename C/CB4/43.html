Money Play. How should Red play 65?
pips Red=102 White=143
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
Red must leave shots so the question is how to do that?

13/8, 13/7 solves the problem of clearing the mid-point but leaves 22 shots. 8/3, 8/2 leaves the minimum shots. 13/7, 6/1* leaves more shots but a) puts White on the bar and b) gets White off the ace-point.

Both these last two plays win about the same percentage of games but as is often the case the hit triumphs because it wins more gammons.
