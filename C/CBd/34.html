Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=11 White=8
This is a double – just. The point is that White doesn’t have a redouble after any roll by Red so Red should double now to increase his equity. Trivial take of course.

If Red owned the cube it would be a different matter. Then he should hold on to it and hope to give a maximally efficient redouble next turn. Holding on to the cube gives him additional chances in those variations where things go badly on the first roll.
