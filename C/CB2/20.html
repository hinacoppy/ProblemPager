Match Play 2-2 to 5. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=26 White=33
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
At this score the double/take decisions are close to the money answers but White has no recube vig so must have 25% or better to take. Does he have that here?

Firstly Red should definitely redouble as he has only four fewer checkers off (five is the benchmark) and if there was no chance of leaving a shot then White would have a big pass. However, Red leaves a shot on 22 of his numbers and then when White hits he has very real winning chances.

Estimating those chances over the board is difficult. White must also factor in his racing chances. I know that with approx. seven hits for White plus the racing chances I would take this and XG supports me. White gives up nearly 0.1 equity points by dropping as his winning chances are 27.5%. Clear redouble and take.
