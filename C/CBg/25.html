Match Play 2-2 to 17. Should Red redouble to 8? If redoubled should White take?
pips Red=133 White=117
Red certainly has the advantage and White’s home board is a mess although it should be noted that technically he has no dead checkers. Note also that White has a healthy but by no means decisive lead in the race.

Red’s outer board checkers are just a bit too far away from the attack zone for this to be a correct redouble. For once taking a roll is correct. If White doesn’t anchor he will have a much more difficult decision next turn and if he does anchor Red will be glad that he held on to the cube.

If Red’s third checker on his 15-pt were on his 6-pt then White would have a scary borderline take.
