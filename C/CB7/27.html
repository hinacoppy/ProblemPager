Match Play. Red leads 1-0 to 7. How should Red play 61?
pips Red=130 White=74
It would be nice to hit White away from the edge of the prime with 5/4* but the gammon cost is just too high.

13/7, 5/4* (not 16/10, 5/4* where the gammon cost is even higher!) wins 2.5% more games but loses 9% more gammons – not a good deal for Red. At DMP 16/10, 5/4* would be absolutely the correct move.

White is not favourite to escape and by next turn Red may have a powerful redouble that will be a borderline take for White.

Red does best to bide his time with 13/6. After all he owns the cube – a very powerful weapon in such positions.
