Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=160 White=145
I actually had this position when trailing 0-2 to 7 when it is a double but only just and it is a trivial take. However, I expected my opponent to pass and he did so fairly quickly.

Red still has three checkers trapped behind a four-point prime and a great deal of work to do to win this position and his threats are just not strong enough to warrant a double in a money game and it is a blunder to double.

Having said that a lot of players will drop this as White so knowing your opponent is critical in positions like this one. Against an unknown opponent I may well venture a double early in a match in a position such as this purely to get a read on his ability to handle the cube.
