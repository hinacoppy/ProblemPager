Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=121 White=126
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
With a close race, Red’s unwieldy structure and White’s strong board it is the double rather than the take that is in question in this position.

Rollouts give double and no double the same equity so this is a position where you need to judge your opponent. If there is any chance that he would drop then of course the double is mandatory. Against most players I would take a roll here. The position is non-volatile and I’d rather give White a more difficult decision later on.
