All those who commented got the right idea here.
White has four checkers off so Red cannot afford to play safe and needs maximum coverage of his 6-pt.
The additional wrinkle is that by playing 13/12 with the ace Red ensures a return shot if White rolls 66.
The correct move is 14/11, 13/12.
