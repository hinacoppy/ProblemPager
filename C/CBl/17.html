pips Red=118 White=145
This is one I found very strange. I played 13/7(2) preferring it over 10/4(2). In this I was correct as it is better by a tiny fraction.

My surprise comes from the fact that the rollout shows 13/10(4) as the very clear winner. It is not often that I am nonplussed by a rollout but I have to admit this one stumps me. Can the advantage of clearing the mid-point really outweigh the ugliness of the resultant structure?

Unfortunately I haven't had time to count shots for each play nor have I had the time to play out the position several times which is my normal approach when I come across an oddity.

For now I am filing this one for a more in-depth analysis when I have the time which won't be for a week or two.
