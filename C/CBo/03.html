Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=131 White=140
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
A classic prime versus prime. As I have said before most prime versus primes are doubles and takes and this one is no exception. The double is clear and actually the take is also very clear despite the lack of an anchor.

If Red’s rear checkers were on the 21-pt instead of the 22-pt, giving him fives and sixes to leap the prime then the position would be a huge pass but, because Red can only leap with sixes, White’s prime is perhaps stronger than it looks.

It is a triple blunder to drop this double.
