Money Play. How should Red play 22?
pips Red=107 White=79
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
Red will have difficulty executing a blitz because of White’s strong home board. He must also be careful about the number of shots he leaves as an early hit by White could cost Red a gammon.

Making the 2-pt is the wrong strategy as it leaves too many immediate shots and it commits Red to an attacking game plan.

The alternative plan is to build a prime and hope White’s board cracks. !3/7 is clear and then the 2 should be played 12/10, creating new a builder for the 5-pt while only leaving a single shot (55) for White. Even 13/11, 13/7 (leaving two shots) is an error.

The key here is to select the right strategy and then attempt to execute it.
