Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=136 White=113
While it is true that Red has some bad numbers because he has three checkers behind White’s mini-prime he also has a lot of gammon potential and of course he has a broken prime.

If we learnt nothing else in 2016 we learnt about the power of the broken prime.

White’s lack of anchor and his other outfield blot means he must pass Red’s double because he loses a very high percentage of gammons.
