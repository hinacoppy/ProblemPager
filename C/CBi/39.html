2014 - Position 241

Match Play. Red trails 0-2 to 5. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=91 White=34
For money this would be a premature redouble and a correct beaver but at the match score Red can comfortably redouble due to the volatility.

For White, if he drops he will be at 50% match winning chances. If he takes and loses he will be at 25% and at 100% if he takes and wins. He is risking 25% to gain 50% and thus needs 33% game winning chances to accept the redouble.

He easily has this as even hitting the second blot doesn't necessarily win for Red and with eight checkers already borne off White is around even money if not hit. In the position White actually has 41% game winning chances.
