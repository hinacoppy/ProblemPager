Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=138 White=152
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
It is more the double than the take that is in question here. Red’s board is imperfect while White’s structure is very sound with no weaknesses other than the two checkers on the bar.

Having said that two on the bar is a substantial weakness and two thirds of Red’s 60% wins will be gammons. That is enough to make this a marginal double. A trivial take for White of course.

When I originally looked at this position the third checker on White’s 8-pt was on his 9-pt. in that scenario the position becomes a very strong double and the take is nearly a blunder.
