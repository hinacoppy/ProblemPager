A classic “pay me now, pay me later problem”.

This is definitely a pay me now position, even for money as White is improving, so now is the time to take a risk with 13/9, 13/11. Playing 6/2, 3/1 or 5/1, 3/1 just postpones the problem of clearing the mid-point.

At the match score the move is even clearer because of the extra gammons it generates when White’s blot on his 2-pt is picked up.
