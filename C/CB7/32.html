Match Play. Double Match Point. How should Red play 21?
pips Red=156 White=121
In a money game bar/23, 6/5 is a reasonable play as Red still has some chances to win going forwards.

However, at DMP Red wants to do everything he can to guarantee his timing for his 2-3 back game. To that end the correct move is bar/23, 4/3* to force White to hit more checkers. At DMP anything other than bar/23, 4/3* is an error.
