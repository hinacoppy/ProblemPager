For money this would be a huge redouble and the skinniest of takes.

Trailing 2-5 to 13 White has a much clearer take of Red’s redouble because of the power of his potential redouble to 8.

Should Red wait to redouble? No, he is not so far ahead that he should be circumspect and if the cube does get to 8 he can use all 8 points. If the score were 6-2 in Red’s favour then he should not redouble! Such is the beauty and complexity of match play doubling decisions.
