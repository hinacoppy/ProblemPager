In the match scenario Red must play to win the game as a loss means the loss of the match â€“ gammons are irrelevant. Deciding on the best play to win the game is very difficult and certainly 9/5, 2/1* comes into consideration, aiming for a well-timed back game with chances to go forward but it turns out that trying to win by going forward immediately with 23/22, 5/1* is best while White still has two more blots.

For money Red must recognise he is likely to lose and should seek to limit gammon losses. He should play 23/22, 9/5. It wins very nearly the same amount of games as 23/22, 5/1* but loses fewer gammons.
