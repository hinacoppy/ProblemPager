Money Play. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=74 White=48
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
This is effectively a last roll problem. If Red hits he will nearly certainly win (and with a small percentage of gammons). If he misses then he will nearly certainly lose (bar a freak hit or a couple of large doubles).

Twenty rolls hit and sixteen miss. Ergo, Red must redouble now to maximise his equity. A trivial take for White of course. White will win with redouble to 8 unless Red has rolled 55 or 66.
