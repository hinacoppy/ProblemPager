Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=150 White=155
This is an excellent reference position.

It should be clear that red should double. He has the stronger position and a number of threats. He is even marginally ahead in the race. Not doubling this would be a bad blunder.

The take/drop decision is right on the borderline with XG rating either action as correct. White has an anchor and all his checkers in play, albeit some of them in ugly stacks. The optimist takes, the pessimist passes.
