Money Play. How should Red play 32?
pips Red=124 White=109
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
The key here is that Red must hit. Playing either 13/8 or 8/6, 8/5 puts you in blunder country. The only question is whether to choose 8/3* or 5/3, 5/2*.

The latter leaves fewer shots but wins fewer gammons – it also loses fewer gammons. In fact the two plays trade off against each other with XG having 8/3* winning the contest by 0.008 so for all intents and purposes the plays are equal.

So long as you hit you got the problem correct – if you didn't hit I'm afraid it's back to school!
