The blot on White's 3-pt gives Red tactical opportunities. With the 3-pt made he would have to play 6/2, 4/2 but here he can venture 17/15, 5/1 or 17/11. The former is the stronger play, generating more shots/hits.

In this position 6/2, 4/2 is a bad error.
