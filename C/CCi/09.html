For money Red must double this position to activate the gammon threat but at this score Red should not double although it is close. He is likely to be leaving a triple shot next turn and so he should hold the cube to use later. At the moment, White has a trivial take. Red needs to give White a much more difficult decision.
Of course, if you think your opponent might drop then, by all means, go ahead and double.
