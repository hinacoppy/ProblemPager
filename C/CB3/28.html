For money this straightforward: the extra gammons gained when Red hits and covers with 8/2*/1 far outweigh any other considerations and any other play is a blunder.

At DMP with no cube and no gammons the pure play 20/14*, 8/7 just wins out over 8/2*/1. At DMP it is often right to keep all the checkers in play and this position (just) demonstrates that stratagem.
