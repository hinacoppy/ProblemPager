Red's position is not as strong as it looks. Most of the time, as he is trailing in the race, he is going to be hitting loose on his 3-pt and White will return hit 30% of the time. When that happens Red will lose a few gammons because he is likely to have additional blots that White can pick up.

All of this adds up to no double/take. To double, thus activating gammons for both players, is a 0.05 error. Red should wait and (hopefully) cash next turn.
