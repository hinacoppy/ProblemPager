This is a big no double/take but I’ve seen many a player drop positions like this because they only see the down side.

Red has two checkers trapped behind a four-point prime. If he hits loose any 1 by White puts him in a strong position and if doesn’t hit any 6 by White probably makes him favourite.

After the sequence 43 for Red (10/6, 4/1*): White fan, White has a trivial take of Red’s double. Those four-point primes are stronger than you think.
