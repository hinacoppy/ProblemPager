Match Play. Red leads 5-0 to 7. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=59 White=83
I posted this to remind people that even at 5-0 ahead to 7 it can be right to redouble and many a match has been lost because of the leader’s reticence to double at the right time.

You need to understand doubling windows and for an introduction to that there is no better book than Kit Woolsey’s “How to Play Tournament Backgammon”.

The doubling window here is 70% to 77.5% (approx.). Red is inside the doubling window by about 2% and so this is a perfectly correct double and take. Don’t be scared to double positions like this.
