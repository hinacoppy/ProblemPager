Money Play. How should Red play 21?
pips Red=164 White=157
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
I think a lot of people would play bar/22 here but that is a little bit of conducting a war on two fronts that I so often counsel against.

Bar/24, 13/11 puts no pressure on White’s 8-pt. The correct play is bar/23, 24/23 locking up the safety of anchor, putting pressure on White’s 8-pt and leaving Red free to develop his forward position next time.

Any move other than bar/23, 24/23 is an error. This is an object lesson in not overplaying a position.
