Match Play. Double Match Point. How should Red play 62?
pips Red=96 White=76
Looks like the quizmaster got one over on the readers here!

Because it is DMP many will have gone for bar/19, 6/4* but that play is a small error. It is not yet time for desperate measures and Red should hold on to his five-point home board.

When I had this position I chose bar/19, 16/14, minimising shots – 19 of them but including a perfect double fives. XG prefers bar/19/17 with 21 shots – why? I believe it is that there is very real duplication of sixes for White (six to hit, six to cover). And the perfect 55 for White after bar/19, 16/14 probably comes into the equity calculations.

What I didn’t expect was that bar/19, 16/14 would be flagged as a blunder! Bar/19, 5/3 is actually rated as much better than bar/19, 16/14.
