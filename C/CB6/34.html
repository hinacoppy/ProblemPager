Match Play. Red leads 4-2 to 7. Should Red double? If doubled should White take?
pips Red=137 White=167
For money this a very clear double and a very clear drop. Red is ahead in race, threat and position.

Things are very different at this score. Redâ€™s double is inefficient because of the overage on gammons. In fact, because an undoubled gammon gets Red to the Crawford Game Red should not double this position and it is a blunder to do so.

For White the potential redouble to 4 with relatively low winning chances gives him a trivial take. It is worse than a double blunder to drop.

Yet another demonstration of the major influence that the score has on apparently sinple doubling decisions.
