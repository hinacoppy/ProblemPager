Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=162 White=159
Surprisingly it is the double that is in question and not the take, which is trivial.

There is real duplication of 3’s and 4’s and Red only has a two-point board. Meanwhile the race is equal.

This is a double, but only just. It is, however, a great practical double as it looks scarier than it is and you will even get the odd drop in positions like this.
