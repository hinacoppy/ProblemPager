It is perhaps remarkable that the pure 7/4, 6/1* is very nearly the right play.

However, for once the prosaic play wins the day and 7/2, 6/3 is the best play. If the cube were in the centre with gammons effectively ruled out by the Jacoby Rule the two plays have identical equities.

Always check where the cube is!!
