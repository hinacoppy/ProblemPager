I had this position against George Jacobs in our Roehampton vs. RAC London League Match.

I’m please to say that I found 8/3*, 8/2 and went on to win game and match.

All other plays give White far too many game winning options. After the correct play White needs a sequence of good things to happen to let him win. Yes – Red will occasionally lose a gammon and the match but that only happens 5% of the time.

8/3*, 8/2 is the clearest route to victory so that is the path to choose. Any other play is at least a blunder.
