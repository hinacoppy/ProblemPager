This position came up in the on-line match between USBGF members and Neil Kazaross. My choice was to play bar/22, 9/5 keeping the fifth point in the prime slotted but this turns out to be a bad error and bar/22, 8/4 is the correct play.

This leaves only two shots at the blot on the 9-pt instead of four at the blot on the 8-pt. Given that Red has no anchor he needs to play conservatively and therefore bar/22, 8/4 is the right approach.

Bar/22, 13/9 is way too loose and is in blunder territory. As noted above Red needs to play cautiously in this position despite the score.
