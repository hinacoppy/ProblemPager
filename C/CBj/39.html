The 1-5 back game is not a great one but here White will end up with an ace-point game at worst and his timing is excellent.

From Red's viewpoint his spares are well stacked but that gap on the 3-pt is annoying. For example with 43 he will make the 3-pt and leave White an immediate shot. However, Red will win a lot of gammons.

All this adds up to a small double and a trivial take. Given the closeness of the decision I would be influenced by the opponent factor here.
