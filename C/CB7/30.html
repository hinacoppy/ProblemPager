It is clear to make the opponent’s bar-point with the 6. Some may play 23/22 with the 1 but better is 24/23, making the deep 23-pt anchor.

Red trails in the race so it is not clear he wants to advance his anchor and secondly White has ten checkers in the attack zone so that should be respected.

With the two anchors secured Red can turn his attention to attacking/restraining White’s rear checkers.

23/22 is a bad error but not quite a blunder.
