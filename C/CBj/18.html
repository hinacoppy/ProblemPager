Match Play. Red leads 9-8 to 11. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=37 White=39
Another of my positions from the UK Open: my opponent had shown erratic cube handling throughout the match so I wasn't sure what he would do here.

Obviously he will redouble immediately but he gets no value from the cube. I judged my two pip lead plus marginally better structure got me sufficiently close to White's 75% take point. In fact I was slightly out but XG estimates that even a 1% chance of my opponent dropping justifies turning the cube. The take is trivial.

I did double and then rolled big numbers to win easily.
