pips Red=86 White=77
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
Thanks to Alastair Woods for his detailed analysis in the comments. He is spot on. At this score is this is a huge double and a very skinny take indeed.

For money it would be double/pass but the fact that White gets to the Crawford Game with a win is just enough to give him a take.

In his comments Alastair mentions Woolsey’s “Encyclopaedia of Backgammon” which should be on everyone’s bookshelf as it is a mine of information. Unfortunately it is out of print and the original proofs have been lost so I am not sure when new copies will become available. My recommendation is to keep an eye on second-hand bookshop sites and hope to snap one up.
