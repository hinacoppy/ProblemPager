Red has the rack but only eight checkers in the attack zone. The race is equal. At 0-0 or 1-0 the threats would make this a good double but an easy take.
At 2-0 Red is better to wait, although it is close. Because you might get a few passes it is worth doubling this if you think your opponent is timid.
