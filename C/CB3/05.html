Money Play. How should Red play 21?
pips Red=108 White=179
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
Firstly Red should hit on the 16-pt as this gives White some crunching numbers such as 33 and 44. The prosaic 1 is 7/6 or 6/5 but the correct 1 by quite some way is 2/1*!!

This stops White having the chance to immediately make the 1-pt and also lets Red recirculate those checkers that are out of position when White hits one of them.

The play may look risky but thematically it is correct. Red is only just over 60% to win even if he makes the best play and with any other move he is 57% or less. The gammon percentage is constant at around 33% after any play.
