Those four White checkers on the 2-pt are ruinous restricting White's flexibility considerably. Move one of the spares on the 2-pt to White's bar-point and this would be a big no double/take.

As it is that weakness gives Red a perfectly correct redouble because of the volatility. Not redoubling is a blunder. Equally well Red can quickly crash his home board and so White has a very comfortable take.
