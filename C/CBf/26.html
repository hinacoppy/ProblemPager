Money Play. How should Red play 62?
pips Red=99 White=121
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
This is another type of position often misplayed.

Over the board Red played 11/3 but that leaves three stripped outfield points and the alarm bells should be ringing.11/3 is a blunder.

Correct is 10/8, 10/4, clearing a point and providing some much needed spares. The old adage ‘a point cleared is a point not be to be feared’ does the trick here.
