Match Play. Red leads 3-2 to 7. Should Red double. If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=118 White=139
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
For money this would be a clear no double and trivial take. White has all his checkers in play and Red's rear checkers haven't moved.

However, at this match score Red's gammons are perfectly efficient and this is enough to make this a clear double although it is a very easy take. It never ceases to amaze me just how much difference a match score can make to a decision.
