Money Play. Should Red redouble? If redoubled should White take?
pips Red=95 White=51
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
Two checkers closed out is close to a double and so logically three closed out must be a pass.

There are variations here where White can anchor and survive but they are few and far between especially if Red knows how to execute trap plays. Even if Red only closes out two checkers he can take additional risks, if necessary, in the bear-off to pick up the third checker.

This is a long way from a take and it is a double blunder to do so.
