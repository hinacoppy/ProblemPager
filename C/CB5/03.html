Money game. How should Red play 62?
pips Red=79 White=165
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
Only two legal moves, 10/2 and 9/1 and yet most people get this one wrong.

The most difficult point to clear is the 9-pt because it held by both of White's anchors and so Red should clear that point now before White's board improves any more.

It is perhaps surprising that 10/2 is worse than a blunder giving up 0.114 equity points! If White's spare checker on his 6-pt were on his 3-pt giving him a four-point board then 10/2 would be correct and 9/1 would be a blunder.

The devil is in the detail!
