Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=163 White=168
Red’s position looks threatening and given the right roll such as 44 he could do a lot of damage but even then, White is guaranteed an ace-point game.

From here the game could take many courses given it is so early.

Positions with only two points made in the attacker’s home board are very rarely passes and this one is no exception. In fact, passing would nearly be a quadruple blunder.

It is the double that is more in question. This is a double but only just because there are only a few market-losing sequences and because White cannot be blitzed.
