Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=22 White=8
I used the EPC count to solves this problem. Red’s EPC is 22 + 5 = 27.

White’s EPC is well-known as 7n (n=number of rolls to bear off) + 1. Therefore, White’s EPC is 28+1= 29.

Based on these figures the position is a strong redouble and a borderline take. XG agrees and this is actually very close to a pass.

Without some sort of formula this type of position is very difficult to calculate over the board.
