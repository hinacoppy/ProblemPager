This position occurred in last weekend's tournament in Texas. The message here is very simple - take what the dice give you!

You could try 22/16, 13/11 or 24/16 or 22/14 all of which have their merits but all of which give white some inviting targets to attack.

The simple 24/22, 13/7 is best and any other play is at least an error. The play creates an anchor, distributes a builder to a useful point and leaves no shots. With the race being very close this is the logical approach.
