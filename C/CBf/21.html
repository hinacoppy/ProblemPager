a) Match Play. Red leads 2-0 to 7. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=119 White=137
b) Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?


I always use look at the money solution first as a benchmark and then adjust for the match score.

For money White loses too many gammons to make the take of the very clear double viable, but it is close due to White’s excellent structure and taking is only a small error. Any weak play by Red could turn this into a take.

At the match score it is still a double but now the take is very clear due to the power of White’s redoubles to 4.
