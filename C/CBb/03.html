However, White’s position will improve and all of Red’s outer board points are stripped. Despite the apparent risk now is the time for Red to hit with 18/17*, 18/16 and it’s not even close.

If White fans, which happens 25% of the time, then White must drop Red’s double. If White does hit there will be lots of return hits for Red. Any play that doesn’t hit is a triple blunder or worse.
