Match Play 2-2 to 17. Should Red redouble to 16? If redoubled should White take?
pips Red=44 White=36
The key in any match situation is first to understand what the correct cube actions are for that score.

I have always used the Janowski Match Equity formula which works at virtually all scores. At this score the arithmetic is simple. Red’s doubling window opens at 50% and closes at 82%. White’s point of last take is therefore 18%. That means he can still take when both players have six checkers left in a no-miss situation.

In this position neither side is likely to miss but five 1’s from Red would do it and not all of his doubles will work all the time, e.g. successive double 1’s.

From all of this it should be clear that Red should wait and double White nearer his point of last take. Red did double and was punished when White rolled double fives at just the right time.
