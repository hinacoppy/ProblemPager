Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=144 White=126
A classic middle game double. Red has the advantage but trails in the race. The position is clearly volatile so it must be a double.

White has a take, although it is no bargain. With all his checkers in play he has several ways to win the game. That flexibility puts him into take territory. Dropping is an error but not a blunder.
