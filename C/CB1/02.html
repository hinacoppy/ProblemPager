Money Play. How should red play 52?
pips Red=103 White=151
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
The top six plays all win very nearly the same percentage of gammons but the two hitting play 12/10, 7/2* and 11/9, 7/2*, win 3% more gammons, turning the non-hitting plays into errors.

Yes things could go wrong after the hit but ideally Red wants to close White out so hitting is thematic. In such positions it is often right to hit unless your opponent has a full prime on his side of the board. There are obviously exceptions to this but as a general guideline it has worked well for me over the years.
