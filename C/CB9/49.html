The key here is not to play 6/4(2)*/2(2)*. This gives better blitz potential but the key point on the board is very likely to be Red’s 5-pt. After 6/4(2) the other two 2’s should be played 8/6 and 13/11, maximising coverage of the 5-pt.

As you might expect in such a strong position there isn’t much to choose between a whole lot of plays but the key is to understand the importance of the 5-pt.
