Money Play. How should Red play 54?
pips Red=178 White=170
This is a fairly rare beast – the double back game.

Neither player has many checkers in the attack zone so a certain amount of liberty can be taken.

Not hitting here would be a terrible mistake. Always remember the prime rule of back games – don’t play them unless you have to! Here Red should try to win by going forwards. The first step is 21/17* and then it is merely a question of the 5.

Maybe the gut reaction is 21/16 but correct is 23/18, playing to make an advanced anchor. As noted above Red should not fear White’s attack and as he doesn’t want to play a 2-pt anchor game now is a great time to get off that point. 23/18, 21/17* it is.
