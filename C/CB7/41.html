b) Match Play. Red leads 3-2 to 7. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?


For money any formula will tell you this is a clear double and a clear take.

At the match score White’s take point (using the Janowski Formula) is 27% and he doesn’t have that so this is a pass. Consider also that any White redouble to 4 will be slower than usual because although it gets him to Crawford it gets Red over the finishing line exactly.

Taking is an error but not a blunder.
