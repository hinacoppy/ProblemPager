Match Play. Red leads 3-2 to 7. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=92 White=68
White should be wary as four points takes Red to exactly the number he requires.

Red will hit with any 1 but not any 6. If White doesn't escape immediately his own board will crash and Red will have even more leeway to hit on the 2-pt. This all adds up to a very clear drop for White and of course Red should redouble, despite the fact that White will redouble to 8 immediately.
