DMP. How should Red play 61?
pips Red=150 White=137
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
I had this position as Red during this week's RAC 32 tournament. I looked at 24/18, 9/8; 12/6, 8/7 and 9/2*. In the end I decided against the bigger plays and went with 12/6, 9/8.

After I had lost I wondered if I had been too passive but it turns out that XG supports my play (just). In a close race the risks from the other plays are too high and after all 12/6, 9/8 keeps all my checker in play with no immediate weaknesses for White to attack.

I was glad I wasn't panicked into either of the weaker plays. In this position White is about 2-1 favourite after any play and unfortunately I couldn't defeat the odds.
