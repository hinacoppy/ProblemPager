pips Red=148 White=133
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
For money 18/8 would be very clear. Can 18/14, 13/7* be correct at DMP?

Red can't lose a gammon nor can he be cubed out. The play leaves 16 shots and would seem to be too dangerous. But those 16 shots don't necessarily win the game and Red gains a lot on the other 20 numbers.

The rollouts have the hitting play winning 1.5% more games and so it is the correct play by a small margin. A surprising result and very informative, demonstrating that one has to have a very different mindset at DMP.
