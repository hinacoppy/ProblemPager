Match Play. 0-0 to 5.Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=100 White=105
A classic case of a really volatile position. Red only wins 51% of the time from but over 60% of those wins are gammons. If Red fans White will win with a redouble.

Red hits just often enough to make this a correct double. White, of course, has a trivial take. In a money game the decisions are the same.
