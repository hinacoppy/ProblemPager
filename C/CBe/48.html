Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=159 White=92
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
The volatility indicates this is a massive double for Red. The question is whether White can take.

At first sight it looks as if White loses too many gammons but a key point is that White has no checkers out of play and he has a surprising amount of what is known as ‘scramble vig’.

That 67 pip lead in the race is huge compensation for all the shots and of course most of the shots aren’t outright winners. Something like 52 for Red followed by 32 for White gives White a very reasonable position.

It is scary to take positions like this but it is actually a blunder to drop this double.
