This can be played safely with 16/14, 15/3 but that leaves future problems. Far better to take a risk now to reach a strong doubling position.

The right play is 13/7(3), 15/9, duplicating fours. 16/4, 15/9, 13/7 with the idea of tempting White of his anchor to hit is a very close second.
