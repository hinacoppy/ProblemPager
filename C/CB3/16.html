Money Play. How should Red play 43?
pips Red=101 White=123
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
At Double Match point it would be a tie between 13/9, 12/9 and making the 3-pt but in a money game it's not close because of the gammons and 7/3*, 6/3 is very clear.

It is six shots instead of two but there is a long way to go and White has a blot on his 3-pt. Move the checker from White's 8-pt to the 3-pt so that the 3-pt is made then 7/3*, 6/3 is a big error.

Always remember to look at both sides of the board!
