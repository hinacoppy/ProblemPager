In this position I played bar/19*/13, 8/2(2)* which is the correct play. Bar/19*, 8/2(2)*, 7/1* is an overplay that is actually a blunder given that White still has a five-point home board.

My opponent anchored on the ace-point, hit a shot in the bear-off, got in an optimal redouble to 8 and won the game. So I lost 8 points having been confident, after my 66, of winning 8 points. Such is backgammon!
