Money Play. How should Red Play 61?
pips Red=168 White=23
I played 23/17, 22/21 when I had this position as Red, trying maximise shots on White’s bad 2’s, 3’s and 4’s.

It turns out that Red should keep the 23-pt but break the 22-pt with 22/21, 15/9. I haven’t counted all the shots in the next couple of rolls but Red gets a lot of multiple shots even when breaking the 22-pt so there is a certain logic in keeping a last ditch defence and the difference on 44 is huge!

More work is required on this to fully understand the position but it’s certainly interesting. 23/17, 22/21 is a 0.063 error.
