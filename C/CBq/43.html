Match Play. 0-0 to 7. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=131 White=40
This type of position is quite common. Red has a double shot to more or less win the game and he will actually win a gammon 17% of the time.

For money this is a big no redouble because the cube is still fully active, and White will use it to win the game if Red misses. At this score however, this is a very clear redouble and it is a blunder not to do so.

If White is missed he will play for the gammon rather than turn the cube to 8 and that makes all the difference. If White cannot use the cube there is no reason for Red to hold it. After all, a gammon with the cube on 4 wins him the match!
