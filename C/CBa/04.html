b) Match Play. Match tied 7-7 to 11. Should Red double? If doubled should White take?


Position submitted by Alastair Woods

At 8-7 Red should play on for an undoubled gammon. Although he looks a strong favourite things can quickly turn sour and White wins from here 35% of the time. So the correct actions are no double and take.

A completely different story at 7-7 where a gammon wins the match exactly for Red. Now it is a very clear double and a monster pass.
