Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=162 White=184
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
A weird and wonderful position. When something like this turns up you need to take extra time to make sure that you gain an understanding of the position.

Over the board Red doubled and White, seeing those five checkers on Red’s ace-point, quickly passed. This is a great practical double because White may well pass but technical it is incorrect.

Red is on the bar and fans with nine rolls. Although those White checkers on Red’s ace-point are worrisome they won’t be staying there long as Red has no home board to speak of. At this point White has many ways to win this game and Red does not have enough threats to warrant a double. Technically the position is no double/take but always remember you are playing a human being and not a bot so attempting to steal a point is fully justified.
