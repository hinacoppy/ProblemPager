Money Play. How should Red play 62?
pips Red=86 White=129
13/11, 13/7 and 13/5 both leave 13 shots. Intuitively it would seem leaving one blot would be best but 13/5, suffers from extended jeopardy – the remaining blot is hard to tidy up.

Couple this with the fact that Red gets more return shots when White hits after 13/11, 13/7 and it should be clear that two down is the correct play. I’ve seen this type of position misplayed many times because of the fear of leaving two blots.
