Don’t give your opponent unnecessary counter-play and don’t jeopardise a cube turn.

After bar/20, 6/5 Red is very likely to have a powerful double next turn. The same is not true after bar/24, 20/15*.

The latter is a double blunder. Always think about the cube and its potential use. This position is a classic demonstration of the importance of that idea.
