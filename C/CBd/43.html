Money Play. How should Red play 42?
pips Red=62 White=106
It is rarely right to “pay now” against a five-point board because there is virtually no life after death.

This position is no exception and 5/1, 3/1 is the right play. Any other play is an error.

Interestingly at a score of 1-5 to 7, 8/6, 8/4 ties with 5/1, 3/1 because gammon losses are irrelevant and gammon wins are important. 8/6, 8/4 wins more gammons because it achieves a quicker bear-off, assuming of course that the blot isn’t hit.
