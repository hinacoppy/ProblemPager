Money Play. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=115 White=75
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
If the cube was in the centre this would be a trivial problem as Red would have to double to activate gammons.

Redoubling is much more difficult question as gammons are already in play. A hit for Red doesn’t necessarily win either the game or the gammon but White does have four blots and the position is highly volatile.

It would be a very timid White indeed who dropped this so psychology doesn’t really come in to it. Technically XG has this as a redouble by a mere 0.011 so I wouldn’t criticise anyone for holding on to the cube here as it is so close. The problem would be if you didn’t even consider redoubling.
