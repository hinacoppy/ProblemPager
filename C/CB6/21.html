Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=145 White=139
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
There aren't many positions where you can have two checkers on the bar, be behind in the race and still have a perfectly valid double.

This position is an exception that proves the rule. The volatility, the gammon threat and the overall weakness of White's structure gives Red a clear double.

White has a very clear take as he can quickly turn the game round and his anchor provides long-term security. Both sides win a fair percentage of gammons from this position.
