Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=148 White=67
This is the denouement of Position 20 with colours reversed.

Things went very badly for White and now he faces a crucial decision. It is clear that Red should redouble â€“ if you didn't redouble this you need to take a good long look at your cube handling.

Red will win a good percentage of gammons from here but White can take precisely because he also wins gammons. If Red enters with a hit he will nearly always win but if he enters without hitting White has a lot of scramble vig and holding the cube is very useful to him.

It is a small error to pass but many would do so.
