Money Game. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=129 White=124
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
I must admit that I gave little thought to doubling this position but our silicon friend, XG, likes the double and views not doubling as an error.

The fact that red wins a high percentage of gammons coupled with the awkwardness that White has in playing some of his numbers makes the position volatile enough to double.

This is the sort of very useful education that you can get by running your matches through XG. Twenty years go I would have missed the double and been none the wiser. I certainly learned something from this one.
