pips Red=112 White=60

Some areas of containment play are XG’s weakness and it one area where XG3 will be much stronger than its predecessor. However, it copes with this type of position very well.

The bad mistake would be 18/14, 15/12, blocking 66 but considerably reducing shots on White’s other 5’s and 6’s. 13/6 is not unreasonable and will allow Red to hit with a 4 next time if White is still on the 2-pt.

However, 18/15, 16/12 is the correct play. It maximises coverage of the outfield at the cost of a 64 hit. 55 for White wins anyway but 66 leaves a double shot!

13/6 is a 0.047 error. Anything else is at least a blunder.
