Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=103 White=98
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
The double is clear in such a volatile position with Red a strong favourite. However, the take is equally clear. A fan by Red followed by a six by White gives him very real winning chances. Of course, when things go well Red will win a gammon which he does 18% of the time. However, White wins nearly 30% of the time.

Note, however, that the blot on Red’s 1-pt is required to make this a take. Put that blot on any of Red’s inner board points and White, without the tactical possibility of a hit there after Red rolls a 2 and hits, must pass the double, although it is close.
