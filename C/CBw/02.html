Money Play. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=62 White=77
It is the double, not the take that is in question here. White is favourite to enter next roll and any hit will be a winner. Red has very few market losing sequences, but the volatility is just enough for Red to venture a redouble.

The take is trivial and dropping would be nearly a triple blunder.
