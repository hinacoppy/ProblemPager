Double Match Point. How should Red play 61?
pips Red=148 White=145
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
The trick is to avoid making the 10-pt and safetying the blots with 16/10, 11/10. At DMP you need to make the checkers work and so making either the bar-pt or the 5-pt is better.

This is an instance where the making the bar-pt is fractionally better because it leaves much better distribution of the spare checkers.

Although this a DMP position actually the same logic applies in a money game.
