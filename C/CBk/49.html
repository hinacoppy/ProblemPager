Match Play. 4-4 to 7. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=152 White=148
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
A deceptive position. Being on the bar makes a very big difference here. For money this is a borderline double/no double decision. The take is trivial, it would be a triple blunder to pass! The race is level and there are many different ways for White to win this game, most commonly from a 3-pt holding game.

The match score changes matters. At 3-away vs 3-away the basic rule of thumb is: “double early, drop early”. That rule is based on a quick look at the match equity table and a few simple calculations.

Now it is a blunder not to double but it is still a very comfortable take. Swap Red’s 8-pt to his 4-pt and the position becomes a pass in all variations – that third home board point makes a massive difference.
