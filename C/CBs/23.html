For money this a close but correct take for White – I hope everybody doubled as Red (ahead in position, race and threat). White’s anchor gives him some measure of security.

However, at the match score, gammons are more useful for Red (he wins a gammon 30% of the time) than for White and despite it being relatively early in the match that fact pushes this position well into drop territory and taking is very nearly a blunder.
