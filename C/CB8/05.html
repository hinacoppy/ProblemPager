Money Play. How should Red play 31?
pips Red=163 White=163
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
It’s easy to play on automatic and move 13/10, 6/5 or 13/10, 24/23.

However, White’s blockade is better than Red’s so Red should certainly use the 3 to split with 24/21. After that he has the choice of 24/23 putting more pressure on White’s checker on the 8-pt or 6/5, distributing his builders. The checker on the 21-pt will be not be in any real danger with White only having eight checkers in the attack zone.

It’s a close call but splitting with both rear checkers, 24/23, 24/21, is the narrow winner. 13/10, 6/5 is a blunder
