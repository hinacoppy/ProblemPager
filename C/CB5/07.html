b) Match Play 0-0 to 4. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?

For money this is no double. Two 1's by Red plus some other combinations mean that Red will win from here just 64% of the time. However, the volatility of the position is such that it is only a tiny error not to double. Redoubling for money would be an error.

At the match score winning a 4 cube gets Red to the Crawford game so now not redoubling would be a small error. Never underestimate the importance of getting to the Crawford Game in match play.
