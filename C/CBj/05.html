Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=117 White=108
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
Things can go badly wrong for Red, e.g. double 3, but all his 4’s and 6’s play well and his 5’s don’t play. This is enough to give Red a clear double.

From White’s perspective those extra checkers on his 2-pt don’t help but he has enough counter-play to make this an easy take.

This position originally occurred with Red leading 3-2 in a match to 7. That makes it a much more difficult decision for White as a gammon for Red wins the match for him with perfect efficiency and the take is right on the borderline.
