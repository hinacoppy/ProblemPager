Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=147 White=137
An odd position where white has thrown a very damaging 66 at the wrong time.

White’s stacks of checkers plus Red’s excellent distribution are enough to give Red a double. White’s lead in the race plus the potential for development give him a very easy take.

The key here is to double now. Envisage the sequence Red 31 followed by White 64 and you can see why now is the right time to double.
