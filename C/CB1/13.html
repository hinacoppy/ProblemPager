The four top plays are all close and none of them is an error. You need to play 11/9, 11/6 to get into error country.

24/22, 6/1* wins the prize by a whisker but 24/22, 13/8 (perhaps surprisingly), 24/22, 11/6 and 13/11, 13/8 are all OK.

24/22, 6/1* takes advantage of Red's stronger board, stops White (most of the time) from building a new point and can lead to a blitz. On the downside it removes Red's last spare from his 6-pt.
