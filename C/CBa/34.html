Match Play. Double Match Point. How should Red play 44?
pips Red=75 White=98
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
If White had a perfect home board then 7/3(2), 5/1(2) would be correct but this is a perfect “Pay me Now” opportunity and 16/4, 7/3 is the correct play.

There is life after death (i.e. being hit) because of White’s open 3-pt and the fact that one White checker will probably still be trapped behind a four-point prime.

7/3(2), 5/1(2) is an error but not a blunder. For money and with White owning a 2-cube paying now is right by a long way.
