The first of many positions from this year's World Championship Finals.

Doug Mayfield was Red and played 5/off, 5/2, got hit and went on to lose a doubled game (after Akiko made her worst doubling decision of the entire match).

When watching the match live with various top players from around the world commenting I was fairly sure the best play was 5/off, 4/1. When the blots are missed there is virtually no extended jeopardy. After either of the other plays there is considerable extended jeopardy. Opinion online was divided between the safe play and the hit.

XG has no such problems. The safe play is correct by a country mile. 5/off, 5/2 is a double blunder and 5/off, 3/off is a triple blunder. Doug paid heavily for his error.
