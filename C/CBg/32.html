Match Play. Red trails 1-2 to 5.. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=157 White=107
The 4-away/3-away score is complex and worthy of a monograph in its own right.

The redoubling window for the trailer is very unusual because it ranges from 18% to 60%. As most people know if the trailer has a match-winning single shot then redoubling is correct.

This position is not in that class because there is a lot of play still in the position.

I was Red against the MCC captain, Tony Lezard, in a London league match last week. I knew I was the favourite but was I near enough to Tony’s point of last take to justify a redouble? Over the board I wasn’t sure so applied Woolsey’s Law and redoubled. Tony took and after some excitement I went on to win game and match.

In post-match analysis XG Mobile on my iPhone said no redouble/take but a long rollout on the full XG has this as redouble/take and Red’s position is strong enough that not redoubling is an error.
