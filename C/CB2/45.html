Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=108 White=152
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
A classic 3-4 back game.

The 3-4 back game works best against an outside prime. Here Red has the advantage of not owning the 9- and 10-pts so it will be easier to bear in his checkers and thus he is better off. Does White have enough hitting chances coupled with some very small racing chances to take?

The answer turns out to be either yes or no! White's equity is virtually identical whether he takes or passes which makes this a very useful reference position. Add it to you library!
