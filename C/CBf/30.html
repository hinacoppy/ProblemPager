Money Play. How should Red play 65?
pips Red=158 White=151
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
A very close call between safetying a blot with 24/13, which keeps Red ahead in the race but leaves an inviting target on White’s 5-pt, and 20/9 which leaves the blot on the 24-pt out of harms way while creating a good builder on Red’s side of the board.

I played 24/13 when I had this position but wasn’t sure and hence its inclusion in the ‘Position of the Day’.

There are advantages to both plays and XG gives a 0.017 edge to 20/9. It is worth remembering this as a reference position for similar positions in the future.
