Money Play. How should Red play 44?
pips Red=167 White=142
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
This is a complex problem which I got wrong over the board, playing bar/21, 13/5, 8/4*.

The problem with this play, and most of the other single hit plays, is that it gives White a lot of hitting numbers in return and quite a lot of double hits of his own.

The best play by quite some way is bar/21, 8/4(2)*, 7/3* which puts two White checkers on the bar. Any reply by White that doesn’t hit probably gives Red a double that White can’t take and a single hit by White gives Red a lot of returns.

Before the advent of the bots this play would not have been made by 99.9% of the playing population. Now we recognise the power of the double hit, especially when White has other loose blots. Any play other than bar/21, 8/4(2)*, 7/3* is a very bad blunder.
