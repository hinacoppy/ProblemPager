Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=168 White=154
This one is perhaps surprising.

Red has the edge due to his four-point prime and White’s somewhat disconnected structure. However, there is nothing much wrong with White’s position. He has an anchor and leads in the race. Other than the checker on his 2-pt all his checkers are working.

The position is not very volatile. A hit for Red followed by a fan for White makes the position a tiny pass but otherwise White is taking next turn the vast majority of the time.

By now you may see where this is leading. This is no double for Red (albeit only just) and a trivial take for White.
