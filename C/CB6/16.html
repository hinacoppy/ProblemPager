Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=147 White=162
The Dimitri Rule says you should never double with a only a one-point home board.

There are exceptions to every rule and this is one of them. This is a powerhouse double and a bare take. White’s lack of an anchor combined with the ugly structure of White’s home board plus three blots to shoot at makes Red’s double mandatory.

I was actually surprised that this was a take but Red has threats rather than long-tem assets and that just makes enough of a difference for White to be able to take.
