31 and 42 are easy, making the 5-pt and 4-pt respectively. Those points are too good to pass up.

With 53 it is a different matter. The 3-pt is too deep too early to be worth giving up the 8-pt and 24/21, 13/8 is correct. Anything else is at least an error.

With 64 making the 2-pt is a double blunder and 13/9, 13/7 is a single (and big blunder), stripping the mid-point and not activating the back checkers when your opponent has started building a prime.
