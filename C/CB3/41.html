Money Game. How should Red play 42?
pips Red=73 White=85
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
Red wants to clear his outside points as quickly as possible but also make it awkward for White to escape.

Sometimes a single blot does the work of a point and here Red can execute a trap play with 9/5, 7/5, which meets both of the criteria above. Sadly the more prosaic 9/7, 9/5 and 7/5, 7/3 give identical equities to the pretty play as White is unlikely to hit any of the blots.
