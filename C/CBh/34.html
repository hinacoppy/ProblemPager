2015 - Position 17

Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=146 White=98
A relatively non-volatile position. I see people give these up as White fearing a crashed board and more checkers being sent back but Red has to move which will give White chances to attack or perhaps hit a fly shot and if he still has a five-point home board a hit will in all likelihood win the game.

That fifty pip lead is very significant. White can withstand a hit and still be in the race. White’s take is trivial (a quadruple blunder to pass) and in fact Red should take a roll before doubling unless of course he thinks that White will pass. It’s only a tiny error to double now.
