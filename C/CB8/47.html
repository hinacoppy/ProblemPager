Match Play. 4-4 to 7. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=137 White=155
For money this is a very clear double because of the volatility but also a very a clear take as the cube has full value to White. Dropping would be a blunder.

At this score things are very different. The general rule at 3-away, 3-away is double earlier than usual and drop earlier than usual. With lots of gammons in the air taking here would be a double blunder. However, Red is not nearly good enough to play on for an undoubled gammon and not doubling this position is very nearly a double blunder.

The position reinforces just how much influence the match score in doubling decisions in short matches.
