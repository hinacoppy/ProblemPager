Match Play. Red trails 0-1 to 7. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=166 White=57
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
For money this is a big no double/take but the match play situation is very different.

At 0-0 or with a lead Red is still quite a long way from a double but the 0-1 score has a powerful impact. Now not redoubling is a blunder which might surprise you.

Both players win a gammon 20% of the time but Red’s gammons are far more efficient than White’s because of the score and Red doesn’t want to lose his market so he must redouble now.
