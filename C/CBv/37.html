pips Red=135 White=105
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
Stop me if you have heard this one before but the match score is once again the deciding factor.

For money and with a fully active cube Red is not quite strong enough to double. However, a doubled gammon gives Red the match exactly and that perfect efficiency makes this a big double and not to double now would be a blunder.

White has a trivial take both for money and at this match score.
