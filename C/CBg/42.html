Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=127 White=131
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
I think that many would take this double as White, based on the strength of their forward position but they would be wrong. It is a blunder to take Red’s very clear double.

If you move White’s blot on his ace-point back to his 9-pt then the position is still a huge double but also a very clear take.

This shows that you have to consider all the elements of a position when making your cube decisions. Who would have thought that blot on White’s ace-point could be so expensive!
