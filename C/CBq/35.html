Red’s only market losing roll in this position is double ones.

So, unless this is already a redouble and a drop this is not the moment to redouble.

There is just too much danger of Red’s checkers on his 2-pt being separated and subsequently hit to justify a redouble here. In fact, Red only wins 59% of the time from here (albeit with 13% gammons) and it is a blunder to redouble.
