If you are going to make a point then the5-pt is better than the 4-pt. Thee issue wuth that approach is the number of return shots plus if White anchors he is still in a good poition.

Better to grab the bull by the horns and hit two checkers with 8/5*, 6/4*. The 21 return shots may spell the end of the game but Red still has a fighting chance depending upon his next roll and whether White entered both checkers or not. On the other 15 numbers Red looks very good indeed.

8/5*, 6/4* is the best play. Making the 5-pt is not an error but it very nearly is. This is a case of “two on the bar is better by far”.
