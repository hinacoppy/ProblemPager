Match Play. Red leads 4-3 to 7. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=67 White=83
Sometimes players are loath to double positions like this, fearing the redouble to 4 and the match then being on the line.

However, that 16 pip lead is huge and Red should be thinking about getting to 6-3 (Crawford) rather than just 5-3. This is a very clear double and an equally clear take. If Red rolls 61, 62 or 63, leaving a direct shot then it is correct for White to redouble to 4 which he certainly wouldnâ€™t be doing for money!

Not doubling is not quite a blunder but it is close.
