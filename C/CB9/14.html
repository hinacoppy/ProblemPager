Red must hit here and I played the ‘obvious’ 13/10, 8/7* but that makes all of White’s entering 5’s and 6’s good for him.

I completely overlooked 8/7*/4. This leaves one new blot instead of two, starts a better point and leaves about the same number of shots as 13/10, 8/7*. It also duplicates the 3 White needs to the Red blot on White’s 3-pt.

Before the bots very few players found hit and slot plays like this one. Nowadays we are much more aware but I played on automatic rather then thinking deeply about the position. If you do that then 8/7*/4 is very clear.
