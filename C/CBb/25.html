Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=113 White=92
A classic 3-pt holding game. Red trails in the race but has two White checkers trapped behind a five-point prime.

White’s board is OK now but may be irreparably damaged by next time hence the time to double is now due to the volatility.

It’s no bargain for White but he has just enough equity to squeeze out a take – it would be an error to pass. This is an excellent reference position. Take the checker from White’s bar-point and put it on his ace-point and the position becomes a big pass.
