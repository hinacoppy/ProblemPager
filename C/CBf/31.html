Match Play. Red trails 3-5 to 7. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=163 White=161
We have seen this 4-away vs. 2-away score before and no doubt we will see it again.

Red has opened with 31 and White has responded with 51. For money this would be a very premature double but at this score any hint of a gammon is enough to turn the cube and not doubling here is a 1.5 blunder.

One thing I have noticed from countless games on GridGammon in the last twelve months is that players are becoming more aware of doubles like these but there are still many who double far too late at this score.
