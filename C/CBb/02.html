Money Play. How should Red play 31?
pips Red=90 White=172
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
White has a reasonably timed back game but it could go wrong very quickly.

Red wants to slow down his own progress and he can kill sixes (other than 61) with 10/9, 8/5.

The other thing to consider when playing against a 1-3 back game is that 3’s can very quickly become a problem once all the checkers have been borne into the home board. Therefore it is wise to have extra checkers on the 5-pt to cope with this as 3’s won’t play off the 6-pt or 4-pt.

10/9, 8/5 also helps with this problem so it is no surprise that it is the best play and that 8/5, 6/5, stacking checkers on the 5-pt is the second best play.
