As you might expect with Rad having such a dominant position the plays are close.

Red can hold everything with 11/5, 6/1 and that turns out to be the second best play.
8/2*, 7/2 wins the second highest amount of games but Red should be thinking gammon here and to do that he needs to trap White off the 4-pt now.

The best play is 11/5, 10/5 which makes most of White's sixes very bad for him, especially given the two home board blots.
