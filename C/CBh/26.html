Gammons are irrelevant to Red and, as he has already doubled, he must win with the checkers.

The only viable play is 21/16, 8/2 (when ahead in the race, race). Strengthening his home board protects him in some measure from the forthcoming attack. 21/16, 8/2 meaningfully triplicates fours.

Even in a money game 21/16, 8/2 would be the correct play.
