Money Play. How should Red play 53?
pips Red=134 White=151
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
The gut reaction is to make the 5-pt but the 8-pt is very valuable to Red in this sort of position as it blocks White’s sixes.

23/15, solving the problem of the rear checker and duplicating White’s threes is the correct move. Note that if White hits the outfield checker he will have to give up a key point (unless he rolls 11 or 33).
