Match Play. Double Match Point. How should Red play 11?
pips Red=73 White=118
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
For money 7/5(2)* would be clear because it wins many more gammons.

However, at DMP gammons are irrelevant and Red needs to make the play that wins the most games. 7/6(2), 4/3(2) wins 2% more games than 7/5(2)* so it gets the nod.

Once you think about the move is clear but in the heat of the moment it is oh so easy to play 7/5(2)*.
