This one generated an argument at the Battersea chouette but it shouldn’t really have done so.

This is a classic ‘pay me now, pay me later problem’ with everything in favour of paying now. If Red doesn't play 13/6, White’s board will improve while Red’s weakens.

Not paying now is worse than a double blunder.

For the record, Red played 13/6 and White rolled 61!
