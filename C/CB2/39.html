Money Play. How should Red play 53?
pips Red=113 White=150
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
It is oh so tempting to make the 5-pt with this 53 as Red.

It is the key point you want to make and with White's board hardly threatening surely you can take the risk and play 10/5, 8/5?

Sadly no: simple safety wins the day and 15/0, 9/6 leaving four shots (and no hit and covers) instead of twenty-one is the play. Red is very likely t be able to make the 5-pt later with much lower risk than after 10/5, 8/5.

Score one for the pragmatism.
