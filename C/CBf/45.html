Pay me now or pay me later? This is a classic position.

It would seem that all the conditions favour ‘pay me later’ and that 6/2, 4/1 is correct. However, Red’s position is so awkward that the ’pay me now’, 11/8, 11/7, might be right.

In fact, the rollouts have 11/8, 11/7 as right by 0.013 equity points. Thus demonstrating that there are occasions when leaving a shot when there is virtually no life after death can be correct.
