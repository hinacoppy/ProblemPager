Money Play. How should Red play 31?
pips Red=150 White=101
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
Logic initially says that 8/5*/4 is the correct play.

However, Red is substantially down in the race. If he makes the safe play and White then enters Red will probably have to hit loose next time. That leads us to wards the correct play, 8/5*, 6/5, leaving the 4-pt slotted. If the blot is not hit Red can rapidly build his board.

It is a risk but now is the time to do precisely that. Remember, Red owns the cube so has the right to play the game to the end.

Surprisingly, 8/5*/4 is very nearly a blunder.
