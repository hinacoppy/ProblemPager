As ever the score is the deciding factor. Red does not get full value for his gammons at this score and White will be redoubling much earlier than usual. As a result, what is a double/pass for money becomes a big no double/take at 3-away vs 5-away.
Doubling is a double blunder. Red should leave the cube centred and play for an undoubled gammon until he sees how things develop.
