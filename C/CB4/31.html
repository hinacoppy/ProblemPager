Money Game. How should Red play 54?
pips Red=90 White=82
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
This position was introduced more as a benchmark than anything else. My rule of thumb is to run in such positions when my opponent has 1, 2 or 3 builders in place and to stay when he has 5.

4 builders is the most difficult case and factors such as the strength of the home board and the race need to be considered.

In the actual position staying with 6/2, 6/1 is correct by 0.024 but if one of Red's checkers on his 6-pt was on the 5-pt, i.e. his board is already damaged, then staying is correct by a big margin. It is only the fact that Red has a perfect home board and the race is close that allows him to consider staying and even then it is slightly wrong. Lesson learnt.
