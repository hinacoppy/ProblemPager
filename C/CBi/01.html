Match Play. Double Match Point. How should Red play 31?
pips Red=100 White=73
The blot on White's 4-pt is the key here. Without that Red would be playing 10/6 but the blot creates tactical possibilities and splitting the rear checkers is the correct play. This gains on White's small numbers and 65 and creates a triple shot on 66!

The three can be played with either 10/7or 9/6 but the key is to recognise the need to split the back checkers.
