Money play. How should Red play 41?
pips Red=164 White=92
Red has the opportunity to hit now but his home board is hardly threatening. Should he try to improve that board and then hope to get a alter shot from his well-timed 2-5 back game.

The answer is a categorical no. He may never get another shot so should take this opportunity now. The simple 23/22*, 5/1, making a four-point home board is the best play. 23/22*/20/16 (the pure play) is nearly a blunder and 20/16, 13/12 (as played over the board) was a triple blunder. Time and again I have seen players wait for the later shot that never happens and the gammon is lost. Take the opportunity while you have the chance!
