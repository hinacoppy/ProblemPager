pips Red=68 White=83
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
For money this is surprisingly close to a take although it is a small pass.

At this score White needs only 17% to take and redouble next roll. His hitting chances are nearly good enough on their own so when you add in his racing chances it is a very clear take.

Passing this double is would be a triple blunder, but I have seen worse drops!
