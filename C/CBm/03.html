2014 - Position 77

Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=18 White=20
Nearly a three-roll position but if either player rolls badly it could be a four-roll position.

In positions like this if the trailer is behind more than a pip then that is normally too much in such a short race and that is the case here. This is a clear drop. Move one of the White checkers from the 5-pt to the 4-pt and it becomes a take.

I recommend reading Walter Trice's Boot Camp to get a better understanding of bear-off doubling decisions. You can also download my “Backgammon Races” monograph from the ‘downloads' section of this website.
