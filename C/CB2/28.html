Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=134 White=149
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
A classic middle game double: Red has a clear advantage in the race and has threats while White has the better home board.

The position is volatile so the moment to double is now. White's anchor and his better board give him a comfortable take as he has many ways to win.

Red doubled, White took and Red then rolled 62. He correctly played 10/4, 6/4 and went on to win a gammon
