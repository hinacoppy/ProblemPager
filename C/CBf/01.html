Match Play. Red trails 0-3 to 7. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=120 White=179
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
For money this is a comfortable take. White’s reasonably timed 1-3 back game plus the small chance that he can win by going forwards would make it a blunder to pass.

At this score however White’s redoubles are somewhat constricted and so are not as efficient as in a money game. Now it is a blunder to take!

Once more the match score is the dominant factor.
