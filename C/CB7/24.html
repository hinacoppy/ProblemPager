Match Play. 1-1 to 5. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=164 White=178
For money, this would be a ludicrously early double.

It’s a different story at 4-away vs 4-away. Now gammons work perfectly for Red to give him the match. That is enough to make this a clear double but also a very clear take – after all gammons work for both players.

Players are loath to double such positions but the more I study with XG the more I have learnt to double in such positions and such doubles reap very rich rewards.
