Until the bots came along I don't think anybody was sure which was the right play: (a) 9/4*, 8/4 or (b) 8/4*, 8/3*, in positions like this.

The former makes the points in order while (b) leaves one less blot.

The blots have resolved this particular debate. Making the points in order is more important than the extra blot. Lesson learnt!
