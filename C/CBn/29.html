Money Play. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=110 White=105
A deceptive position: Clearly Red should redouble. What is not so clear is that White has an easy take.

Even if Red does make the full prime he has two checkers of his own stuck behind a four prime and his own prime may not last (for example, if Red's next two rolls are 64 and 43). If Red hits loose, e.g. with 32 played 6/1*, then any 1 by White gives him very real winning chances.

It's a scary take but dropping is a blunder
