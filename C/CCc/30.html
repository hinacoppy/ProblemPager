Unlimited Games. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=109 White=85
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
After double/take Red has positive equity because of his gammon wins. Not doubling is a quadruple blunder. He must double now to activate gammons (note with unlimited games the Jacoby Rule is in effect).
Note that even if Red fans White does not have a redouble.
