Money Game. a) How should Red play 66? b) if the cube is in the centre how should Red play 66?
pips Red=151 White=174
With the cube in the middle you have a choice between the pure play 16/10, 13/7(2), 11/5 and the aggressive 13/1*, 8/2(2)*. The former may give White a more difficult decision with the cube next turn. After that the latter play White's decision is likely to be much clearer. For that reason alone I would vote for the pure play. XG has an equity difference of only 0.006 between the two plays.

With the cube on White's side of the board any play other than 13/1*, 8/2(2)* is a double blunder or worse. Red must play to maximise his equity by winning a high percentage of gammons and the double hit is the only move that plays to that game plan.
