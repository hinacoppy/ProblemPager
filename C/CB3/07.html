This position is from Falafel (Red) vs Mochy in the London Open Super Jackpot.

It is just too easy to routinely make the 5-pt but that gives White every chance to make an advanced anchor and a well-timed back game. That play would be an error.

The dynamic play and one found quickly by Falafel (he is after all the world number 3 giant) was 6/5*, 6/4*. This play denies White the immediate high anchor (unless he rolls a double) and simultaneously unstacks Red's ugly 6-pt. The play succeeds spectacularly when White doesn't hit one of the two blots and Red can start eyeing up gammons.
