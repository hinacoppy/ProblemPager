Money Play. How should Red play 63?
pips Red=127 White=116
This one is a slight puzzle to me.

I played 14/8, 9/6 and had had it flagged as a blunder by XG. It rates 14/5 as far superior.

14/5 reduces the number of shots from 4 to 3 and does give return shots after White hits with 35. In addition, 14/8, 9/6 means that Red owns both his 2-pt and 8-pt and they can’t both be part of the same prime. Finally Red’s 1’s and 3’s (which don’t play well on White’s side of the board) may play better after 14/5 but that’s a little tenuous.

Are those differences enough to warrant a 0.114 equity difference on the rollout? Apparently so, but I’m still surprised.
