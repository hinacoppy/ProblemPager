For money bar/20*, 13/3, 8/3 would be very clear.

At this match score gammons are particularly valuable and so bar/20*/5* should at least be considered. Over the board, being optimistic, I went with the double hit. I was pleased to find it was only wrong by 0.021.

The key is to consider the play even if you elect not to make it. As fate would have it my opponent rolled 61 and I cruised to the gammon and the match.
