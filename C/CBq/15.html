Match Play. Red trails 2-3 to 7. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=95 White=146
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
This position looks like a drop because of all the space behind the White anchors for Red to dump checkers in. However, White can play this as a well-timed 4-pt holding game (with a few extra chances) where Red has an open 5-pt.

This and the fact that he loses only around 10% gammons gives him just enough equity to take. Of course, Red must double and many players of the White checkers would pass, as happened over the board.
