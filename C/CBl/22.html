For money this is a clear no redouble and a very easy take. Red has too few market losing rolls to justify a redouble especially with an active cube and gammons counting.

At this score however a redouble kills the cube and gammons so Red just needs to be a good favourite to redouble. He has 65% here so it becomes a clear redouble and take. Not redoubling is a blunder.

Once again, the difference between the match play decision and the money decision is huge.
