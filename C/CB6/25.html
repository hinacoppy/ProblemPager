Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=132 White=145
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
Red is ahead in position, threat and race. On the other hand White has reasonable structure and he does own his 5-pt.

Is that good enough to take Red's very obvious double? The answer is, not quite. By a very small margin dropping is correct because Red wins a gammon nearly 20% of the time and that is just too much.

The opponent factor may make it a take but Red's position is very easy to play so I wouldn't expect too much there.
