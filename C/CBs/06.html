Over the years I have seen many positions like this accepted on the basis that White will enter and then Red will have difficulty escaping his rear checkers.

The prosaic truth of the matter is that Red wins 70% of the time and more than half of those wins are gammons. It is worse than a triple blunder to accept the redouble.
