Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=112 White=130
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
Red certainly has the edge and at first sight White seems to be in trouble but Red is also stripped everywhere and his position may weaken next turn unless he rolls well. He has very few market losing sequences and should wait before doubling. Doubling is a double blunder.

Interestingly if you move the third checker on the 2-pt back to the 6-pt then the position is very close to a double. A remarkable change effected by giving Red just a little bit of flexibility.
