Red can preserve his anchor with 12/1 but it looks remarkably ugly and how will he win from there even if not hit?

Even with the best play Red is only a fraction over 50% to win the game so he must accept that and find the best move to win the game. That move is 23/12 which admittedly leaves 2's, 4's, 5's and 6's to hit but few of those are immediately fatal as he will often have a take of a double after a hit by White and a fan by red. 23/12 keeps all Red's checkers in play and puts the pressure on White to perform.

The second best move is 23/18, 7/1 but that is still a bad blunder.
