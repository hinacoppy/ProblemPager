An interesting position and thank you for all the comments.

The bots have taught us to be much more aggressive than we were twenty years ago and this position is a graphic illustration of that idea. The fact that White has only a four-point home board and has three blots on his own side of the board makes the double hit with 6/3*, 4/2* absolutely mandatory.

12/9, 4/2* is the only other move that is not a double blunder!

Playing Red one must consider the glass is half full and not half empty. A bit of optimism works wonders at backgammon! Remember gammons count double!
