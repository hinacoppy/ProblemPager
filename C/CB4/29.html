Hopefully it is clear that the first two fours should be 8/4(2)*.

Given that Red doesn't many checkers in the attack zone, 11/3* is too big a play with only 1's, 9's and 18's to cover. Red must bring more checkers to the zone and 13/9(2) is the right move by a big margin.

22/18(2) is not aggressive enough but it is the move Kikiko chose over the board.
