Money Game. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=113 White=142
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
Clearly Red should double as anything that clears the mid-point loses his market by a long way.

I was playing White. I have always distrusted the 5-pt/bar-point holding game and so I let this one go but in fact White has just enough chances to take. The combination of racing and hitting chances and the fact that White loses very few gammons give him enough equity to take.

This position therefore adds another useful reference position to my library.
