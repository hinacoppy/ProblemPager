Match Play. 0-0 to 15. Should Red redouble? If redoubled should White take?
pips Red=99 White=106
Position submitted by Leo Waters

For money this is a clear redouble and take. Even if Red hits the 4-shot he still has a blot on his 3-pt and his home board structure isn’t great. However the volatility is such that he should redouble.

Holding the cube on 4 in a 15-point match Red should be more cautious. His redouble to 8 activates the 16-point gammon for both players and White’s redoubling point to 16 is quite low (around 18%).

That should set the alarm bells ringing and the rollouts have redoubling and not redoubling as a dead heat so the final decision is the opponent factor. If you think he might drop then redouble in a flash!
