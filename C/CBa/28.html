Money Game. How should Red play 62?
pips Red=134 White=143
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
As a quiz problem this is relatively easy and most people find 24/16. Red is in danger of being primed and it is more important to leap the prime than to make the 4-pt. There is some duplication of 3’s and 4’s.

Over the board it is not so easy and many will play 6/4 with the 2 and then spend time agonising over which 6 to play, not realising they have already gone wrong. How did you do?
