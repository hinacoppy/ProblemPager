Match Play. Red leads 4-2 to 7. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=120 White=107
I made the classic error here of making a money decision in a match and dropped Red’s double. For money this is double and drop. White loses too many gammons to allow him to accept.

At this score, however, Red does not get full value from his gammons (the concept of overage) plus White will need only 15% winning chances to redouble. These two factors give White a skinny take. Obviously it’s still a very strong double.

Not doubling is a huge blunder.
