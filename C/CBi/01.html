Money Play. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=62 White=131
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
A scary but correct double and a scary but correct take. The position is incredibly volatile so Red can’t just sit on the cube - White must be made to pay to see the game continuation!

Equally well a fan by Red and six by White (13.5% chance) and it is White who could be winning the gammon!

Not redoubling is a triple blunder. Dropping the redouble is merely a 1.5 blunder!

Now imagine that as White you are in the box facing five opponents in a chouette and they all redouble? How do you feel? This is why I always say that in a chouette if you can’t write a cheque for 200 times your nominal stake and sleep easily you are playing for too much. Wide words I believe!
