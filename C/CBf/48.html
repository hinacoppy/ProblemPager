Money Play. How should Red play 41?
pips Red=123 White=112
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
I went badly wrong on this one, playing 13/12, 9/5 in an attempt to create builders for new points. This is overplaying the position in style!

The key point is that White’s position is at breaking point with some of his 5’s being truly terrible. Red should not be creating any targets for his opponent at precisely the moment when his position is about to break.

While 9/8, 9/5 is acceptable the very simple 13/8 is best, giving White nothing.

After my 13/12, 9/5 White rolled 62 (24/16*) and went on to win the game. That 62 would have been awkward after 13/8, and look at the difference if White rolls 35!

This is a powerful lesson that sometimes doing nothing is absolutely the correct plan. I learnt a very useful lesson here.
