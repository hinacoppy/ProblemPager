Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled. Should White take?
pips Red=103 White=96
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
It looks as if White is about to crash and burn BUT a fan by Red followed by a six for White which happens about 21% of the time puts a very different perspective on things. If you couple this with the scenario where Red enters and hits but then can’t escape while White languishes on the bar, then you can see that White’s winning chances are very real.

In fact, Red wins from here about 37% of the time but of course he does lose a fair percentage of gammons (16%).

Putting all of this together gives a technical no double and trivial take. However, I would often double a position like this in practice, particularly against an unknown opponent, as you will get a fair number of passes.
