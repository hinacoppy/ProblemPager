Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=86 White=70
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
This is one of those odd position where Red should double to avoid losing his market on a small number of market losers, any 7 plus 66 and 55. Importantly the double activates Red’s 10% gammon-winning chances.

The key point is that White won’t be able to redouble any time soon because of his horrendous structure and even if he misses this shot Re may well get more. It is an error not to double here.
