Money Play. How should Red play 54?
pips Red=151 White=156
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
A choice of game plans for Red.

He can try the blitz attack with 11/7*/2* or he can try the priming option with 15/10, 11/7*.

In both variations he may have trouble escaping his rear checkers. The blitz attack leaves 12 return shots while the priming play leaves 14 but the priming play is stronger because if Red does make the prime it may help him crash White’s home board as White will have four checkers trapped behind Red’s prime.

The blitzing play is a small error but the key is to understand why the priming play is the stronger option.
