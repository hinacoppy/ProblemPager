This one can solved by reference positions.

Five checkers off is the benchmark for taking after being hit in the bear-off but that assumes the hit player will never miss once he brings the hit checker home again. That is not the case here as White will miss with 3â€™s.

However, Red does not yet have a closed board and that is a bigger influencing factor. That actually makes the position no double/take but only just and I would not criticise anyone for doubling this in the hope his opponent might pass. XG says that if there is a one in five chance that White will pass then the double is valid.

This is a case of knowing your opponent (or not).
