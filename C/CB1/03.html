Money Play. How should red play 21?
pips Red=145 White=137
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
Discounting 11/8 that does nothing to improve Red's position, the choice is between 13/11, 6/5 (14 shots) and 13/10 (7 shots).

Are the extra shots (plus the asset of owning the 11-pt) and the possibility of making the 5-pt next turn justified?

Yes but only by the smallest of margins. Note that White needs fours to hit and fours to cover his 2-pt so there is some useful duplication.

The key here is to consider both plays. If you didn't even think of slotting your 5-pt then you need to rethink your approach to such positions.
