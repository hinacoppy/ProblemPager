Match Play. Double Match Point. How should Red play 21?
pips Red=93 White=47
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
At DMP you need to make use of all the checkers – you cannot win with the cube.

Red needs to keep his rear checkers back and spread out so they shouldn’t move. To win the game red will need his 5-pt so 6/5 is more or less forced. The choice then is between 8/6 and 6/4 for the 2.

In a money game 8/5 would be clearly correct because it loses fewer gammons but at DMP 6/5, 6/4 edges out 8/5 by 0.006 because it utilises the checkers slightly better. In fact either play either play is fine.
