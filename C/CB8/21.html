Match Play. Red leads 9-8 to 11. How should Red play 31?
pips Red=23 White=93
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
3/off, 2/1* takes off an extra checker but there is immediate future jeopardy plus the potential for exposing a second checker with an entering 51 if hit.

4/1, 2/1* is right by a long way as it removes all the delayed jeopardy. If Red is hit he does not have a double from the bar because of the match score. For money he would have a double.

I had this position in the British Masters. My opponent played 3/off, 2/1* and I rolled 44! Such is life!
