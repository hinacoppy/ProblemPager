Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=152 White=149
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
Red has very good structure while White does not and those two White checkers on Red's ace-point are stranded. White also has a blot subject to six hitting numbers. The race is in the balance.

The position is quite volatile so it cries out for a double. White has enough chances to venture a take but it is closer than you might think. Not doubling is a blunder.
