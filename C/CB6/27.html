Match Play. Red trails 0-2 to 5. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=92 White=85
Obviously for money this is no double and a beaver but once more the match score influences matters hugely.

It is very close but the high percentage of gammons that Red wins just makes this a double although it is very close. Getting to the Crawford game is sufficient incentive for Red to double.

Doubling when the underdog is something you just have to get used to at match play. Technically it is a very small error not to double as Red.
