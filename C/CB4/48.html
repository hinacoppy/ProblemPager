I had this position on Gridgammon. I doubled and my opponent dropped. No surprise and on we went to the next game. Then came the post-match analysis.

Firstly for money it’s an easy take because of the problems Red has safetying that rear checker plus the problem of clearing of the 8-pt plus the racing chances.

The real surprise is that at this score XG rates this as no double, albeit only just. It looks as if because White’s redoubles to 4 work with perfect efficiency (winning the match for him) that Red should first safety that checker on the 15-pt before doubling. An interesting result I think you will agree?
