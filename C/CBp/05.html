For money not doubling here would be a clear blunder. White has no anchor so is at risk of being blitzed.

At this score, Red needs to be wary of the cube coming back on 4 if the attack fails. White's four-point prime is a potent threat if thing go well for him. That fact is just enough to make this technically no double.

However, this is great practical double. A lot of players will drop this cube, scared of losing a gammon and going 0-6 down.
