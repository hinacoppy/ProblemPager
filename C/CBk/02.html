Match Play. Red trails 1-5 to 11. How should Red play 22?
pips Red=99 White=40
Kikiko (Red) had doubled prematurely in this game which is Position 175 a few rolls later.

Red needs to pick up the second checker if at all possible. She also needs to put White on the bar if possible and one option is 6/2, 3/1(2)* but Red does better to give White two hitting chances with 6/4, 6/2, 3/1*. This maximises the chances of picking up the second checker. 11 is White's only good roll.

The prosaic 6/4(2), 6/2 is a borderline blunder. Akiko went on to win the game.
