Match Play. Red leads 3-1 to 11. How should Red play 52?
pips Red=93 White=98
This is an easy problem provided you see the play. It is too easy to play 7/5, 7/2 assuming there is nothing better. However, the bots have taught us to look further than just “routine” moves.

6/4*, 6/1 is the right play by a long way. A fan by White puts Red back in charge and the possibility of a gammon is once more in the wind. 20/15, 6/4* (the full banana split) is way too big a play just in case you considered it!
