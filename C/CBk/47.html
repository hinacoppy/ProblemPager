For money this is only just a redouble as the cube is still live after the redouble.

That is not the case at this match score as a redouble puts the match on the line. White has a simple decision â€“ does he win more than 25% of the time in which case he can take.

As Red has only taken off four checkers fewer than White it should be clear that White has his 25%. Five fewer checkers off is normally the guideline for such positions. In fact White has nearly 27% winning chances so the solution is double and take.
