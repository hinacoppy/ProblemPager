This is not the time to split the rear checkers. That could give the initiative to White unnecessarily and it gives him good 3’s for no reason.

13/6 does nothing so the logical move is 13/8, 6/4, attempting to improve Red’s prime and significantly improving Red’s distribution. The position should be played as a prime versus prime.

Any move other than 13/8, 6/4 is a blunder.
