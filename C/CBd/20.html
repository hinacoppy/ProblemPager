Match Play. Red leads 1-0 to 7. Should Red redouble to 8? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=46 White=137
It depends upon which match equity table or formula you use what you think White’s take point is here. I use the Janowski formula which gives White’s take point as 17%.

Does he have that here? Yes he does, by about 3%, so the take is easy. The question is the double. By redoubling Red cripples his gammons and he wins a gammon and the match about 47% of the time from this position. He does better to hold the cube and play for the gammon. Redoubling is a small error.
