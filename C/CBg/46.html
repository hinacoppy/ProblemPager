This position demonstrates just how much difference a checker on the bar can make. Put that checker on White’s ace-point and the position becomes a massive pass.

However, White still has the better home board and if Red fans White’s numbers are beautifully diversified. Of course Red’s broken prime with four checkers behind it is a powerful influencing factor.

How does all this add up? Red still has a double but it is close. However, the volatility demands that he should double now. White ha a trivial take, dropping would be nearly a quadruple blunder!
