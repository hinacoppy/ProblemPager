Match Play. Double Match Point. How should Red play 31?
pips Red=60 White=137
With gammons counting 4/off would be correct.

However, at DMP all you are concerned about is winning the game. To that end clearing the 6-pt early is important and then after that, not surprisingly, the 5-pt.

With this roll Red should play 6/3, 5/4, preparing to clear both points. Other plays are close, as you would expect, but it is understanding the principle that is important.
