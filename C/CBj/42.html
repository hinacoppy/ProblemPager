Money Play. How should Red play 11?
pips Red=107 White=60
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
7/6(2)*, 4/2 is easy to miss but it is the correct play by a long way.

Firstly, White leaves an additional blot if he enters with 22 or 23. Secondly, and most importantly if White fans he has to drop Red’s redouble whereas, after any other play, he can take the redouble, but the threat of Red’s board being closed immediately after 7/6(2)*, 4/2 makes it a drop.

Never forget the cube and its impact when looking at candidate plays!
