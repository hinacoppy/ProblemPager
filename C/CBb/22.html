This position came up in my match against Sean Williams in the London League match between Roehampton and the Union Club.

For money this would be double and a very easy take but at 3-away/3-away the rule of thumb is to both double early and drop early. I doubled and after long thought Sean passed.

In fact it is a take but only by 0.014 equity points so in practice either decision is OK. The big error would be in not doubling as Red.
