Red should try to squeeze every last drop of equity from this position.

4/3, 4/1* is clear. If the blot is hit he might be able to pick up two more White blots. 4/1*/off is very nearly a blunder.

For money the two plays have virtually identical equities but here a gammon wins the match for Red so no contest.
