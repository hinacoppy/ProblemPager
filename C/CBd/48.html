Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=79 White=73
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
Technically Red is behind in the race but White has a lot of wastage so Red isn’t really behind.. Equally well White is forced off Red’s 4-pt with any 6. This adds up to a double for Red as the position is very volatile and he could have lost his market by next turn.

Can White take? He can win the race but also by hitting a shot. At the moment his board is strong enough to be a threat to Red and that is sufficient for him to take. Move one checker from White’s 4-pt to his 3-pt giving him only a three-point home board and the position becomes a big pass.

My rule of thumb for these positions is that the defending side needs at least a four-point home board to take.
