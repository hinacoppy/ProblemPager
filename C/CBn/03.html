Money Game. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=132 White=107
That Red should double this position is obvious as he has plenty of threats.

What perhaps is not so obvious is that White has a very comfortable take. He has a four-point home board, an enemy blot contained behind a broken five-point prime, and is comfortably ahead in the race. Even anchoring on Red's 4-pt gives him good chances.

I think this position would fool many as indeed it fooled me. This position occurred in the Battersea chouette and all four team members as Red dropped the double.
