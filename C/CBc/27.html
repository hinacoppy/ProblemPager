Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=112 White=115
Red certainly has the advantage and the position is very volatile so the double is clear, especially as it activates gammons.

However, like many prime vs. prime positions, this is effectively a six-rolling contest and White has very real winning chances. Even if Red does roll a 6 and escapes a checker (except 62 when he will make his bar-point) White can still win by attacking the remaining rear checker and then rolling his own six(es).

Passing here would be worse than a triple blunder!
