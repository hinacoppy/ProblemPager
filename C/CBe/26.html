This is one from the Battersea chouette.

The team attempted to win by going forward with 13/3* but this is way too ambitious. Sixes don’t grow on trees and Red’s plan should be to try to maintain a well-timed ace-point game and hope to win with a later shot when he has a strong board.

The correct play is 24/14. This wins about 5% more games while losing about the same percentage of gammons.

13/3* is overplaying the position and hoping for too much. As such it is an error.
