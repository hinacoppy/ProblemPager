2017 - Position 46

Money play. How should Red play 54?
pips Red=76 White=73
My rule of thumb in these positions is I don’t mind giving up one home board point but I don’t want to give up two. That works in this position to perfection.

The correct play is 18/9 by quite a long way. 6/1, 5/1 is a blunder because it weakens the home board and gives up too much in the race. It also leaves Red with the problem of what he is going to do next roll when, If White hasn’t rolled a six, most of Red’s fives will play badly.

White cannot double after Red moves 18/9 as he trails in the race and is not favourite to hit.
