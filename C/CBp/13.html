pips Red=138 White=33
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
The 4-away vs. 3-away score is one that generates interesting redoubles because Red can redouble as a big underdog.

In this position if Red doesn't hit a shot he will in all likelihood lose a gammon and the match so he is not risking a huge amount by redoubling. If Red runs a checker from the 22-pt and White then rolls 52 or 62 (and 42 is not good either) then Red may well lose his market after a hit. There will very likely be further shots to come.

It is close but what is an obvious beaver for money becomes a small redouble/take at this score. Remarkable!
