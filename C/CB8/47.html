This is a classic middle game double. Red has market-losing sequences so should double now.

From White’s perspective he is in the race, has a four-point home board and his mid-point holds the Red checkers on Red’s 18-pt. If he can survive the blitz he will have good game-winning chances. In fact he wins about 35% of the time but also loses a gammon about 30% of the time.

Not doubling would be a triple blunder while passing the double would only be a double blunder!
