Match Play. Red trails 0-2 to 5. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=137 White=52
The more you play tournament backgammon the more you realise just how much influence the match score has on cube decisions.

For money this not quite a double but at this score this a huge double – in fact it’s a double blunder not to turn the cube. White has a take of course. A gammon for Red (an 8% chance) gets him to 4-2 Crawford.

My rule of thumb for back games at his score is that a double shot normally merits a double.
