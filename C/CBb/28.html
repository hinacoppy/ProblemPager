Match Play. Red leads 3-2 to 5. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=7 White=7
White needs exactly 25% to take this double. Over the next two rolls he will win 323/1296 times, 1 win shy of the 324 he needs to take. However, sometimes there is a third roll after the sequence Red: 21, White: any non-winning number. The fact that Red might then roll 21 again gives White his take equity.

However, more important is the opponent factor consideration. If White is the weaker player he should snap this up. If much stronger then he should pass.
