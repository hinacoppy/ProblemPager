Money Play. How should Red play 62?
pips Red=161 White=150
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
I ‘routinely’ played 24/18, 13/11 here and was surprised to find it was nearly a blunder. Yet another reminder not to play on automatic.

24/18, 13/11 gives White too many options. When you have the better home board as Red does here it is often better to try to improve that board. 24/18, 13/11 creates three blots. 13/5, on the other hand, leaves only one blot and slots the very valuable 5-pt and it is the correct play by a long way. Even 24/18, 6/4 is better than 24/18, 13/11.

I learnt something very useful from this position.
