Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=13 White=8
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
If White owns the cube he will have some very efficient redoubles when Red rolls something like 31 or 32 but except after 21 Red will have a take.

Equally well Red has a lot of market-losing rolls now including the sequences where he rolls an average roll but the White rolls 31 or 32. All of these factors are difficult to weigh over the board. XG has this as a borderline double and a trivial take.

However, I have seen many a player pass positions like this as White so against all but the best the position demands a redouble. This gives White the opportunity to make a huge mistake.
