2015 - Position 236

Match Play. 3-3 to 7. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=144 White=71
In a money game this is a long way from a redouble with gammons active for both players.

It is very different at this match score. A redouble removes gammons from the equation and so it is purely a question of game winning chances. At this score White has a very high take point of 33% because he can drop and play from 3-5 behind which is 33%.

Does he have 33%? Not quite and so he should let this one go although many would take this over the board. The key is in knowing your match equity tables.
