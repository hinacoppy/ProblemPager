At Double Match Point gammons are irrelevant and so the pure 13/3(2) is just fractionally better than the normal 8/3(2), 6/1(2)*.

A lot of people know this and play 13/3(2) as standard but I thought I would post it here for those who were not aware of that fact.
    1. Cubeful 2-ply    13/8(2) 6/1*(2)              Eq.: +0.050
       0.525 0.167 0.005 - 0.475 0.125 0.004
    2. Cubeful 2-ply    13/3(2)                      Eq.: +0.050 (-0.000)
       0.525 0.165 0.006 - 0.475 0.125 0.005
    3. Cubeful 2-ply    8/3(2) 6/1*(2)               Eq.: +0.035 (-0.016)
       0.517 0.192 0.005 - 0.483 0.131 0.006
    4. Cubeful 2-ply    13/3 8/3 6/1*                Eq.: +0.027 (-0.023)
       0.514 0.170 0.006 - 0.486 0.132 0.006
    5. Cubeful 2-ply    13/3 6/1*(2)                 Eq.: -0.025 (-0.075)
       0.488 0.157 0.004 - 0.512 0.142 0.007
