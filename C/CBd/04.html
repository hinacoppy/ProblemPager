Money Play. How should Red play 64?
pips Red=160 White=137
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
Black must view this as a prime versus prime position. Slotting the prime with 13/7, 10/6 is completely wrong.

Correct is the apparently anti-thematic (racing when behind) play of 21/11. This brings a valuable builder for the bar-point and escapes a rear checker. With only ten checkers in the zone White is not really threatening an attack and that checker on his 1-pt may cause him problems.

Any play other than 21/11 is a blunder.
