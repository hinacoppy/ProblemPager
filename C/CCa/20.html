Match Play. Red trails 1-3 to 5. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=164 White=164
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
4-away vs. 2-away gives the trailer maximum cube leverage because a doubled gammon gives him the match exactly. Study has shown that you can cube extremely early with any hint of a gammon.

This position occurs after the players have traded 21s. White has lost his anchor and so, amazingly, this is already a double and it is nearly an error not to double. A trivial take of course.
