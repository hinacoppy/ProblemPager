Unlimited Games. Should Red double. If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=153 White=164
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
A classic initial double. Red has the advantage in race, threat and position but none of those advantages is crushing. In addition, Red has only eight checkers in the attack zone so this should be a take, which it is.
In fact, it is a comfortable take. Not doubling is a blunder as is passing as White. Take a Red checker from the mid-point to make his 8-pt and the position becomes a massive pass.
