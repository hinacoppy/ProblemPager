This is another position where Black would rather not leave a blot but has no choice. Perhaps the most natural-looking play is 13/8 13/7, but following the general principle that a blot on the 1pt should be covered at the first convenient opportunity lest it become a liability later, 8/2 6/1 leaves about the same number of shots while covering the 1pt.
Note that 14/8 6/1 also covers the 1pt but leaves a double shot in the outfield; otherwise, it would be a contender (see Variant position below).
