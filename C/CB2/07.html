The blitz is an area of the game where the bots have considerably improved human play in the last twenty years.

In 1994 I'm fairly sure I would have played 11/5, 7/5. However the bots' first rule of blitzing is quite simply “never give your opponent the chance to anchor” so 6/4* is clear. Their second rule would probably be “bring more wood” and thus 13/7 is considerably better than 24/18, the back checkers can escape once the closeout has been completed.

13/7, 6/4* is the play. Making the 5-pt is a clear error as is 24/18, 6/4*.
