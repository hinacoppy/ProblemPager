There should be a list of backgammon rules that have been created since the advent of the bots.

If such a list existed one of them would be: “never allow your opponent the chance to make a second anchor when he has a potential back game”.

Following this rule you would play 6/2*/1 and you would be right by a long way. Any other play is at least a double blunder.

If you know the rule you will ‘see' the play. If you didn't even consider the play then you have a bit of learning to do!
