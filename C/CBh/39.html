pips Red=153 White=107

Behind in the race, Red must attack and so 13/5* is mandatory. The question then is whether to make Red’s 4-pt or White’s 5-pt.

“When on attack, attack; when defending, defend” is an oft quoted backgammon adage and here it would serve you well. This is no time for half-way measures and 13/15*, 8/4(2) is the play.

24/20(2), 13/5* is nearly a double blunder and 24/20(2), 11/7, 8/4, which is way too passive, is also a very big blunder.
