Money Play. How should Red play 53?
pips Red=96 White=127
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
There are really only two choices here: 16/8 or 16/13, 16/11.

16/8 leaves one blot and 17 shots (although White won’t hit with 33). 16/13, 16/11 leaves two blots and 14 shots.

Players are often loth to leave two blots but here shots matter and also if missed, it will be relatively easy to tidy up the two blots. After 16/8 Red may not be able to safety the blot on the 16-pt next time – he will need to roll a 7, 8 or 10.

Interestingly, because of that last point, 16/8 is a blunder. 16/13, 16/11 is the correct move.
