Money Play. How should Red play 52?
pips Red=153 White=143
There is little to choose between the passive 13/6 and the more aggressive 8/1*. White’s excellent structure means he is about 65/35 favourite after either play.

The key is that the doubling cube is still in the centre. White doesn’t have a double after either 13/6, or 8/1* but if Red elects for the more aggressive 24/22, 6/1* then White has a perfectly correct double.

24/22, 6/1* is the correct play at DMP but here either 8/1* or 13/6 should be chosen to avoid the cube.
