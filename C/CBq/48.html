Match Play. 0-0 to 7. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=49 White=116
This is a classic position where many will take a roll, but the volatility is sky-high and Red must redouble now. By next turn he (or White) may well have lost his market.

Yes, there are numbers that leave a double shot but White will only be about even money to hit and he has a blot on his 2-pt. On the upside there are gammons to be won - more than 13% of them.

It is an easy take for White. Not redoubling is very nearly a blunder. Even if the extra White checker on his 5-pt was on his 2-pt this would still be a redouble.
