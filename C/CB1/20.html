When in doubt double a blitz position!

With thirteen Red checkers in the attack zone the alarm bells should be ringing for White. Red is going to win a lot of gammons from this position, so much so that White must drop the double.

There will be times when White's home board, coupled with Red's partially trapped rear back checkers, influences matters but this doesn't happen often enough. White should just let this one go and move on to the next game.
