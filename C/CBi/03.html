Money Play. How should Red play 41?
pips Red=97 White=111
This is a simple technical play.

Red shouldn't lift the blot with 3/2 as he has a lot of work still to do and gammons to win!

It's easy to make the relatively safe play 13/9, 10/9 and overlook 13/9, 4/3 that gives many more covers and (hopefully) makes the game a one-shot proposition. If White doesn't roll a 4 Red should be home free.
