Money Play. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=91 White=70
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
This type of position is well covered in Woolsey’s Encyclopaedia of Backgammon. The critical thing is which of Red’s home board points is open.

The four-point is high enough that White’s racing lead is relevant. If it were the three-point that was open this would be a huge pass. I recommend reading Woolsey’s book to get an excellent set of reference positions.

As it is this is a huge redouble and a skinny take.
