Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=135 White=128
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
Years ago when I played at the Double Fives Club in London we always obeyed the Dimitris Rule: “Never double without a new point in your home board.”

In the main this rule works well but this position is (just) an exception. Red's structure is excellent and he holds a defensive anchor to boot. All this makes this a marginal double. Of course White has a very easy take. However 25% of Red's wins will be gammons.
