Money Play. How should Red play 42?
pips Red=86 White=79
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
13/11, 5/1 is better than 5/1, 3/1 because then the spare checker on the 2-pt still has somewhere to go.

The key question is whether Red can risk 13/7. On the plus side it is a builder if White runs with a 6 and it duplicates White’s 3’s. On the minus side the blot could be hit and Red may never roll again.

This is a true case of balancing risk with reward and it’s not easy. I wimped out with 13/11, 5/1, giving too much value to White’s four-point board, but 13/7 is correct. My play was a small error.
