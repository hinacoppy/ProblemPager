Money Play. How should Red play 52?
pips Red=25 White=120
Did you play 6/1, 3/1 to protect against a second blot being picked up after the first one is hit? In that case you made a blunder.

Far more important is to take off another checker with 6/1, 2/off. That play wins more games and more gammons. The parlay required to pick up the second checker is too remote to worry about. Having seven checkers off rather than six is much more important. This position is simply a lesson in priorities.
