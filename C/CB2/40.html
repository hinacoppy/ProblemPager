Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=138 White=101
Position from Phil Simborg

Most people would double this position but not many would take, seeing only a crashed home board and a gammon lost.

The double is clear and it is nearly a blunder no to double. However, it is a triple blunder to pass!

White has many ways to win. A Red fan followed by a White 1 puts tremendous pressure on Red to perform. Red could enter and the crash his home board and then it is he who is looking at the gammon loss. Also, don’t discount White’s long-term equity from holding the 2-pt.

The old adage that nearly all prime vs prime positions are takes looks close to the mark!
