Money Game. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=165 White=151
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
White's four blots and Red's three-point home board point one in the direction of a double but Red has very few checkers in the attack zone and is unlikely to be able to blitz White.

The more likely scenario is that Red will hit a checker and White will make an anchor leading to a more static position. It turns out that this is not a close decision with not doubling being clearly correct.

Of course if you think White might take fright and drop the cube then by all means ship it across. According to XG you need a 12% chance that White will drop to make the double correct.

Only you know your opponent(s)!
