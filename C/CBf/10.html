Match Play. 4-4 to 7. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=105 White=113
At this score white should drop earlier than for money but even so it is a very easy take.

When Red doesn’t roll a 4 he has to compromise his own position. However, for Red there is life after death (i.e. a hit) whereas for White there is very little because of Red’s near perfect board.

All of this adds up to a very big double for Red. Not doubling is worse than a triple blunder! The position is very volatile so it so screaming out for a double.
