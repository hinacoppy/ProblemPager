This is an exercise in continuity and the key is to avoid making the 3-pt with two of the fours. That play separates Red’s army.

Best is 24/20, 16/12*, 13/9(2) where the two checkers on the 9-pt block White’s checkers and serve as a useful landing point for Red’s checkers as they come around the board.

Red has still got work to do to win this games so he needs to use his checkers efficiently.
