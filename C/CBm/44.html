2014 - Position 36

Money Game. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=129 White=120
Red should not redouble but only because he is already too good. In fact redoubling White out is also OK because the equity difference is tiny. Of course if you think White might hallucinate and take you should redouble immediately!

The mistake would be to wait because you don't think you are good enough to redouble.

It is an interesting exercise to take the spare checker on White' s 2-pt, move it around the board and see how its location dramatically affects the redoubling decision. I'll leave you to do just that!
