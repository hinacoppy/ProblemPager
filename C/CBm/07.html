Match Play. Red leads 1-0 to 5. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=135 White=123
For money a double here would be premature as there are only a few market losing sequences so Red does best to leave the cube in the centre. A double would be nearly a blunder in XG terms.

At 1-0 to 5 things are different as a gammon with the cube on 2 wins the match for Red with perfect efficiency. With a good sequence of rolls a gammon is certainly on (in fact Red wins a gammon 22% of the time).

This cube efficiency makes the position a double at this score. White, of course, has a very easy take.
