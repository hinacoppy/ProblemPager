Over the board I would have doubled this but the rollout shows that a double is premature. A fan by Red followed by a 4 for White happens often enough that Red should hold the cube, although it’s close. Note that after the sequence above White will have a redouble to 8 that Red can take unless White has made his 2-pt.

With the cube in the centre it’s a different story as Red needs to double to activate his gammons.
