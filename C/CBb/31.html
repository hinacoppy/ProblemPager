Money Play. How should Red play 54?
pips Red=133 White=113
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
Red’s key objective here is to save the gammon. To that end he needs to minimise the number of checkers he has behind White’s prime.

He can leave a shot later if he has to but for now 6/2, 6/1 is the prosaic but correct play. With a fourth checker back he will be even money to lose a gammon. A fifth checker back makes things very much worse indeed.
