Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=132 White=140
Nobody is going to drop this as White but can it really be a double?

Technically the answer is yes but only by 0.01 and so you would be perfectly within your rights to double but I think most people would wait a roll.

It is interesting that a slightly better structure and a small number of threats can add up to a double in an apparently virtually equal position. Every detail matters!
