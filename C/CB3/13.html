Money Play. How should Red play 44?
pips Red=170 White=69
This is a lesson in backgammon strategy. An army fights best when its soldiers are together rather in two separate camps.

It is too early to make the 2-pt, a move that does split Red’s forces. Red’s plan should be to make his bar-pt, 8-pt and 7-pt.

Both 22/14(2) and 22/18, 22/14, 13/9 play to that plan. 22/14(2) wins the rollout by a whisker but the key here is to understand the correct strategy and then implement a plan that supports it.
