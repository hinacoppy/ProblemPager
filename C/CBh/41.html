Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=130 White=159
A classic middle game position. Red has the advantage but Whiteâ€™s position is sound with no checkers out of play although the three checkers on the ace-point are a worry.

The position is volatile and so red should double. White will lose quite a few gammons but still has enough chances to take.

Note that were White ahead in a match at most scores this would be a drop and even at 0-0 to 7 the take becomes borderline rather than easy which it is in a money game. Losing four points in a seven point match is to be avoided!
