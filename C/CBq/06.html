Red could play safe here with 21/17(2), 7/3, 5/1 but then how does he proceed? There will be a lot of problems in bringing the remaining checkers home.

He does much better to play 21/13(2), despite leaving a direct shot. If the shot is missed he will have a lot easier job bringing home the checkers. 21/17(2), 7/3, 5/1 is an error.

Nack Ballard pointed out that if you advance Red's anchor to the 20-pt then the safe play and the running play are virtually equal. It is interesting that advancing the anchor one point can make such a difference.
