There are times in life when you have to make the really ugly play, and this is one of them! Any move other than 6/5, 6/2 is a blunder.

At worst you will leave a shot later so in this instance you should wait for later as a hit by White will very likely be fatal.

After 6/5, 6/2 you will win about 72% of the time, i.e. nearly two-thirds. Not a bad return from such a terrible position.
