If Red had rolled 51 then 14/8 would be clear, giving him a good distribution of builders.

However, 52 is awkward. After 14/7 it will be difficult for Red to improve and so he should consider slotting with 14/12, 9/4. Even if White hits he will not escape (unless he rolls 61) and his other number may prove awkward to play on his own side of the board.
