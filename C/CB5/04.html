pips Red=135 White=154
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
Without the Quiz Factor I think most people would play 18/10(2)* and indeed I didn't give the position a second thought when playing it.

However, 18/14(2), 8/4(2) and 18/10(2)* both win around 80% of the time but 18/14(2), 8/4(2) wins enough extra gammons to make it the right play, if not by much. 18/10(2)* is not an error in XG terms but it is an incorrect play.

I included this position just to remind you not to play on automatic – even ‘obvious' plays are sometimes wrong.
