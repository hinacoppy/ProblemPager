pips Red=161 White=172

This is one I always used to get wrong. I would play bar/20, 21/15 with the idea of keeping the blots connected and duplicating threes.

The problem with that is that increases the number of double hits for your opponent plus the fact that that one of the hits might be on the 20-pt, a point your opponent would love to make.

You do better to play the simple bar/14 (duplicating twos) and avoiding the problems above. Bar/20, 22/16 is very nearly an error, in XG terms.
