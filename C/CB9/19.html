Money Play. How should Red play 52?
pips Red=144 White=136
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
After your move what is the correct cube action for both players?
Red must hit here – he doesn’t have a safe play anyway.

8/1* is one option and after that play Red just has a take of White’s double but it’s skinny.

The correct play is 7/5*, 6/1* which creates a very volatile position but one with much more potential than the single hit. A bad roll from White and Red can win by going forwards. 8/1* is not aggressive enough and is worse than a triple blunder!

After 7/5*, 6/1 White must still double but now Red has a trivial take.
