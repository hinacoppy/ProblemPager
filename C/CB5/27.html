Match Play. Red trails 2-5 to 7. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=123 White=160
Once again White would have a take for money but a take here is ruinous with nearly half White’s losses being gammons. White’s prime is fragile (look at an entering 61 for example).

A take by White here is a triple blunder and of course Red must double. Hopefully the constant repetition of this type of position is helping you to develop your match play doubling skills. Beware of gammons when leading!!
