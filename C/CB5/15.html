Money Play. How should Red play 43?
pips Red=97 White=132
This is about maximising gammons by recirculating checkers.

6/2, 5/2 is safe but 5/2, 5/1* is the best play as it nets 3% more gammons while winning very nearly the same percentage of games.

Note that White still wins about 16% of the time from here which some might think rather high but that’s what makes backgammon so much fun. White’s position looks perilous and yet he still wins one game in six!
