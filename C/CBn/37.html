Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=134 White=153
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
A classic early double from the bar in a very volatile position. Not doubling here is a double blunder.

However, White has a very comfortable take – a double blunder to pass. He has good anchoring possibilities even if that might be on the ace-point. On four of Red’s rolls (66, 44, 46, 64) White is very happy indeed.

Put Red’s checker on the bar on White’s 2-pt and the position becomes a massive pass. Being on the bar severely diminishes Red’s threats – a point often forgotten when receiving doubles like this.
