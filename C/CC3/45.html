Match Play. 6-6 to 13. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=68 White=121
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
A massively volatile position. Red could win or lose a gammon.
The market loss after a six is huge so Red must double (he’s not too good). Not doubling is worse than a double bunder.
Equally well if Red doesn’t quickly roll a six his position will self -destruct and White can easily win a gammon. Dropping is nearly a double blunder as well. So, big double, big take.
