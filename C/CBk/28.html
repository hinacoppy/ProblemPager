Money Play. How should Red play 43?
pips Red=54 White=64
As pointed out in the comments there is no safe play so how and where to leave the blot?

Any play that doesn't hit White gives him additional racing chances so 6/3, 4/off and 6/2, 3/off are both week.

5/2, 5/1* doesn't take a checker off and also puts yet another checker on the 2-pt. That leaves 4/1*, 4/off as looking like the most sensible play and indeed it is the correct play. It bears off a checker, puts White on the bar and if he enters on Red's 4-pt then Red will have a relatively easy time continuing his bear-off.

Any other play other than 4/1*, 4/off is a blunder.
