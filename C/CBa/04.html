Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=160 White=179
This position demonstrates the resilience of an anchor.

White’s position is no bargain but he is in the game until the end, no matter what, and that gives him a borderline take of Red’s mandatory double. If you didn’t double this highly volatile position then you have some serious studying to do!

Passing is not an error and you may need to factor in the relative strengths of the players when making your take/pass decision.

Note that if this position occurred at 0-0 to 5 in a match then White should drop. Losing a gammon and letting your opponent get to Crawford is just too risky.
