Match Play. Red leads 2-1 to 7. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=65 White=64
For money it would be a blunder not to double. The volatility is sky-high and gammons abound.

At this score Red needs to be a little more careful because the value of gammons to White is greater to White than Red (less overage). However, the volatility still demands a double (and of course a take).

At this score it is only an error not to double rather than blunder bit if you are not doubling such positions you are being far too conservative.
