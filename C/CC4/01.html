Match Play. Double Match Point. How should Red play 21?
pips Red=151 White=145
For money there is virtually no difference between hitting two checkers or hitting one. The extra gammons balance the extra losses.
At DMP hitting one checker with bar/23/22* is clearly correct because it wins the most games which is all that Red is interested in. Hitting just the one checker means that White cannot make a second anchor and that reduces his winning chances.
At DMP hitting two checkers is an error (just).
