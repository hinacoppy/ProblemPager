2016 - Position 222

Money Play. How should Red play 32?
pips Red=74 White=70
My rule of thumb for such positions is that you should not give up the fifth home point board. Going from six points to five is OK but going from five to four rarely is. This is because it is very difficult to contain a hit checker with only four points (your opponent is favourite to enter).

My instinct here would be to play 9/4 and that is supported by the rollouts where 5/3, 5/2 is quite a bad error. My rule of thumb has stood me in good stead over the years.

However, the converse applies so that if White had a five-point home board (move the spare blot on his 4-pt to his 5-pt) then playing safe is correct by a long way.
