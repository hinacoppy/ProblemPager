An old backgammon adage tells us to make the point that is more difficult to make.
Here Red will get later chances to make his own 5-pt but very few to make his opponent’s 5-pt. QED he should make White’s 5-pt, especially as White has that extra builder on his 9-pt.
This is not close. Hitting is a bad error while making his own 5-pt is very nearly a blunder.
