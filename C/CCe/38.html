Match Play Red trails 5-6 to 7 (Crawford). How should Red play 61?
pips Red=17 White=86
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
If you have read Bob Wachtel’s “In the Game until the End” you will know that for money there is little difference between 3/off, 3/2 and 3/off, 2/1*
However, this is the Crawford Game with Red 2-away and now 3/off, 3/2 is a triple blunder. Hitting gives White four fanning numbers which could well cost him a gammon.
Luckily, those who commented got this one correct.

       0.913 0.435 0.007 - 0.087 0.000 0.000
    2. Cubeful 2-ply    3/2 3/off                    Eq.: +1.450 (-0.247)
