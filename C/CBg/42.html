Money Play. a) Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=119 White=75
b) If Red has the cube on 2. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?


White’s position is terribly disjointed but he is not that far from home. Red’s home board is so strong that he can venture a double before White exposes a blot(s).

This activates gammons for him. Even so the double is close and White has a trivial take. Owning the cube gammons are already activated and Red does better to wait until he has a more specific threat. If he does redouble of course White still has a trivial take.
