A deceptive position at first sight. Red will hit if he can but a return hit by White puts Red in gammon danger. When Red doesnâ€™t hit he has five blots to tidy up and far too much work to do.

Red will win a few gammons of his own but not enough to justify turning the cube here. It is, however, only a small error to double and if there is a 5% chance that White will take then the double is valid. Believe me, I have seen doubles like this dropped and so knowing your opponent is important in positions like this.
