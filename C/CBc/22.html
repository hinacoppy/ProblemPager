Money Play. Should red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=134 White=105
With a lot of stripped points and his imperfect home board structure the alarm bells should be ringing for White. He is going to lose a high percentage of gammons.

From Red’s perspective all his checkers are in play, the position is volatile and he could well have lost his market by next turn. The racing deficit should not put him off doubling here.

In fact, White’s position is so wretched he must pass. Taking is an error but not a blunder.
