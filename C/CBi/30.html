Money Play. How should Red play 51?
pips Red=162 White=163
It is perhaps surprising that 21/16 is the correct 5. For the 1 it is close between 6/5 which gives very real duplications of 4's and 24/23, which freezes White's 8-pt to a certain extent.

13/8, 24/23 is an error but not a blunder. If White is to hit it is better to be hit in the outer board. It also removes 11 and 42 as jokers. This theme repeats itself often enough in the opening that you should be aware of it and employ it.
