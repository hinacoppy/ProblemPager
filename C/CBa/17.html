Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=92 White=86
Over the board my opponent quickly passed but actually this is a trivial take and a double blunder to pass.

Red has to both cover his inner board blot and escape his rear checker. If his home board were closed then this would be a pass (see Kit Woolsey’s ‘Encyclopaedia of Backgammon’ for the relevant reference positions).

The double is very clear due to the volatility of the position.
