Match Play. Red leads 2-1 to 5. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=108 White=119
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
This is the converse of some recent match play positions.

This is a simple 3-pt holding game and we all know such positions are doubles and takes. That would be true if this was a money game. It would be a strong double and a clear take.

However, the score is crucial. Two points get Red to the Crawford Game with White on an odd number of points which is very bad for him. He should drop this double very quickly and play from 1-3 down.

Taking is worse than a double blunder.
