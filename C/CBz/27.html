This one cost me a few pounds! I decided it was right to go and played 20/15. My opponent rolled 33 and gammoned me! But was my play correct?

No, it was not. It is rarely right to give up a forward anchor when leading the race. Red would rather go from the rear anchor. Here he cannot do that so he should play the simple 6/1 or 5/1, 4/3. 20/15 is very nearly a blunder. Lesson learnt! (At least until the next time).
