This is really a pay-me-now-pay me later problem.

13/5, 12/4 is slightly ugly but leaves no shots. 7/3(2), 5/1(2)* leaves two shots and an awkward position. 12/8, 7/3(3) solves most future problem but leaves three immediate shots and 13/9, 7/3(3) leaves four.
