This one generated a huge debate on Facebook (and on this site for which many thanks). Red (Doug Mayfield) redoubled and Akiko took fairly quickly. The second checker was hit but she ended up anchoring on the 2-pt and thus saved the gammon.

I did 10,000 rollouts on this and at the end of all those, the decision was a dead heat. XG has White giving up 0.004 equity points by taking. Would I have taken this as White given that a gammon loss means losing the World Championship? Probably not and especially if I thought I was the stronger player, I wouldn't want everything riding on this one game.

White's winning chances are 29% but the gammon losses ruinous.
