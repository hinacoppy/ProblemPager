A very difficult problem.

Can Red really try for a blitz attack or should he be taking his opponent's 5-pt and then working out what to do with the other two 2's? Over the board I couldn't decide and so went with 7/5(2)*, 6/2 and was rewarded with a closeout a few rolls later after some judicious doubles.

I subjected this to a very long rollout and the answer was a dead heat between my play and 22/20(2), 7/5(2)* - it seems that for once conducting a war on two fronts is acceptable. 24/20(2), accepting the 3-5 back game comes a distant third. With his ace-point already made Red will have a hard time winning a back game from this position.
