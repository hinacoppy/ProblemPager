Money Play. How should Red play 65?
pips Red=155 White=144
Position submitted by Grant Hoffman from New Zealand

The “standard’ play in this type of position is 13/7, 6/1* but here that is nearly a blunder. Why?

13/2 is a do nothing play so the real choice is between 13/8, 13/7 and 13/7, 6/1*.

13/8, 13/7 leaves 24 shots as opposed to 18 with the hitting play. However, it has the merit of only leaving a single blot. 13/8, 13/7 nicely develops the stack on the mid-point and it also produces very real duplications of White’s 3’s.

The final point to note is that White’s 4’s play well after the hit but not so well after the pure play. Taking all these points together makes 13/8, 13/7 the correct play by a wide margin. Playing on automatic many would choose 13/7, 6/1* but here that is the wrong game plan.
