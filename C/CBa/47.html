Match Play. Red trails 0-1 to 7. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=134 White=112
This is a brilliant example of the importance of the match score.

For money this is a redouble and a take. White’s dead checkers on his 1-pt and the fact that a lot of his moves will be forced all contribute to making this a volatile position and a double and a take.

At this match score Red’s gammons are very efficient indeed. So much so that it would be nearly a triple blunder to take! Reverse the score and have Red leading 1-0 and it becomes a blunder to redouble!

A lead of 1-0 might not seem much of a lead but when the cube gets to four in a short match strange things begin to happen. Bank this one for future reference.
