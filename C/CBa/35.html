2016 - Position 88

Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=65 White=64
White needs 20 pips to bring home his outfield checkers to 3 pips for Red – advantage Red.

White has at least 5 pips of wastage (3 pips for the 3rd, 4th and 5th checkers on his 2-pt and 2 pips for the 3rd checker on hisace-point). Red has only 2 pips wastage (3rd and 4th checkers on the 2-pt). Advantage Red.

White has gaps on the 5-pt and 1-pt while Red has only a gap on his 6-pt that may well get filled – Advantage White.

The bottom line is that Red, despite a 1-pip racing deficit is just into his doubling window. White has a trivial take.
