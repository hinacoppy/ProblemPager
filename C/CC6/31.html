This is difficult because of the lack of Red checkers in his home board but also very easy because it turns out that all the plays are very close.

On a basic level XG likes bar/24, 6/5(3)* which looks a bit weird but, on a long rollout, bar/22, 6/5* wins by three milli-points over that other play. Also acceptable are bar/23, 8/7(2) and bar/24, 8/7(2), 6/5*.
