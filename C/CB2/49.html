It is easy to play this positionally with something like 13/11(20, 6/4(2) or 24/22(2), 6/4(2) and twenty years ago such plays would have been commonplace.

The bots think otherwise and very quickly start a blitz attack with 6/4(2), 3/1(2)* while White has two more exposed blots. When White fans Red wins the game immediately with a cube turn.

Any play other than 6/4(2), 3/1(2)* is a double blunder.
