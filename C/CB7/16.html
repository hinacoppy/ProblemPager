This is a close call between 22/15, duplicating three, and 9/6, 9/5.

The problem with the latter play is what happens next turn? It is better, albeit not by much, to run now and keep the 9-pt rather than hope for a good roll next turn. It is relatively rare to leave a voluntary shot against a five-point home board but a gammon loss is irrelevant at this score.

22/15 wins approximately 1.5% more games than breaking the 9-pt. White is a slight favourite after either play.
