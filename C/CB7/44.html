pips Red=150 White=54
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
I had this position at the Battersea chouette and fairly quickly played 20/15*, 8/5 maximising covers for the 4-pt but it turns out that 20/15*/12 is better.

It provides some indirect covers for the 4-pt and crucially provides extra outfield coverage as well as the bar point may become important. 8/5 takes a checker that may belong on the bar-point to the wrong place.

I was surprised that 20/15*, 8/5 is ranked as a 0.060 error by XG so I found the position very educational.
