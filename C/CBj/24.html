Money game. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?

A classic case of an army completely split in two and unable to regroup. Red effectively controls nearly the whole board.

Despite the race being equal and Red not having made even one new home board point this is a massive drop. Every time White escapes a rear checker it will be sent back so the racing count is not be believed. White's game plan is predicated on rolling a few large doubles plus hoping his four-point board can have some influence - not a good plan.

Two players in the Battersea chouette took this and got gammoned for their troubles.
