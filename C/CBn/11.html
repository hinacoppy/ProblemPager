Now is not the time to hang back. Red must make a break for freedom. He cannot allow White the time to develop his position without pressure.

After 24/21 the ace should be played 8/7. This distributes the spare checkers nicely and if White does point on Red he will be better off giving White the 4-pt rather than the 5-pt.

8/7, 6/3 is a double blunder. Backgammon is about pressure. 8/7, 6/3 applies none.
