Match Play. 1-1 to 5. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=133 White=92
For money redoubling here would be a double blunder because White has full value from owning the cube.

At this match score, however, his take point is 33% and he gets no value from owning the cube and gammons don’t count.

XG has this right one the borderline of take/pass so it would probably come down to an assessment of your opponent whether you took or passed as White.

I had this position in the Barry Bigplay Memorial tournament. I didn’t redouble (nearly a quadruple blunder). I rolled 64(!), won a single game but then lost the match. Lesson learnt.
