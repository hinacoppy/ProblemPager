Money Play. How should Red play 62?
pips Red=97 White=115
Red is well ahead in the race and his priority will be clearing points not making them, except of course his 4-pt.

If he plays 14/6 he may well have trouble clearing both his 8-pt and his bar-pt later on. He does much better by clearing the troublesome bar-pt now with 7/5, 7/1.

Knowing when to switch from priming to clearing is a necessary skill required to play top class backgammon. 14/6 is an error (just).
