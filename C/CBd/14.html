Money Play. Should Red redouble? If redoubled should White take?
pips Red=83 White=96
One from the Battersea chouette: Red was playing for a gammon when White entered with 14, played bar/24, 18/14*. What now?

Red’s gammon chances when he hits with a 5 plus the fact that he has a five-prime against White’s last checker makes this a very clear redouble. Equally well it is a very clear if scary take for White with lots of redoubling potential.

Sadly I was White and Red rolled 53 - c’est la vie!
