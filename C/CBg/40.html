Match Play. Double Match Point. How should Red play 52?
pips Red=70 White=156
A classic ‘pay me now or pay me later’ problem. 10/8, 10/5 or 7/5, 7/2?

After 10/8, 10/5 most of the hitting 4’s also cover the blot on White’s 5-pt but even if that happens Red still has life after death due to his huge racing lead.

I had this position online and just couldn’t decide between the two plays. Eventually I went with pay now, 10/8, 10/5 and went on to win the game.

The rollouts show the two plays virtually dead-heating although technically I was right by 0.001 points of equity! This is therefore a good benchmark position.
