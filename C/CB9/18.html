Technically this a borderline double and a trivial take. Another position that is an advocate for the broken prime – this time on White’s side of the board. Red has a lot to win this position and White has many ways to win.

A key factor is that Red has only a two-point home board at the moment. Give him another point and the position becomes a drop.
