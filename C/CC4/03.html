Match Play. Red leads 9-4 to 13. How should Red play 51?
pips Red=145 White=183
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
For money one of the hitting plays would be correct but here Red won’t be doubling for a long time and if White gets a back game, he will be able to take for a long, long time. Therefore, Red’s best game plan is not to give White the opportunity to create a back game.
Red should simply play 23/18, 15/14. Every other play is at least an error.
