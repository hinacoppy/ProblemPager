b) Match Play. Red trails 3-4 to 7. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?


For money, this is just a double and a trivial take. With a fully active cube White has many ways to win and his racing lead is substantial, although because of his dead checkers many of his moves will be forced.

At the match score the double is much clearer and it is nearly a double blunder not to double. The fact that a doubled gammon wins the match for Red makes this a very efficient double indeed.
