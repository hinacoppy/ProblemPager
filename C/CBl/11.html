In the rollout 21/16 is the clear winner with 21/17, 5/4 rated as a small error which surprises me.

If White had a five-point board (i.e. his ace-point is made) then you might expect the balance between gammons and wins to change significantly but it doesn't change quite enough to make 11/6 correct. 21/16 would still be the right play.
