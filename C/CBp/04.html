This was originally posted by Phil Simborg and demonstrates just how careful you have to be accepting doubles when 2-away vs. 3-away.

This is very early in the game but the correct actions are double and drop. It is an error to take. White's take point at the start of the game is 29% and the gammon threat makes this a clear pass.
