Match Play. Red trails 0-2 to 7. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=153 White=106
For money, this is not quite a double but trailing 0-2 makes a huge difference. Now not doubling is a 1.5 blunder. The take is still trivial of course.

The reason is that White will be slow to redouble to 4 as if does so Red will only need to generate 10% winning chances to spin the cube to 8. This reinforces how even a small lead in a short match can radically affect the cube handling.
