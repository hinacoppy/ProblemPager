Money Play. How should Red play 61?
pips Red=77 White=112
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
It might seem that 8/1 is correct to prepare for a pick and pass if White runs one checker.

However, correct is 8/2, 7/6. This keeps Red even on the two outer board points and prepares to be able to clear one of them next turn depending upon the dice rolls.

8/1 is an error.
