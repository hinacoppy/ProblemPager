2018 - Position 79

Match Play. Red leads 3-2 to 7. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=97 White=121
Obviously, this is a clear redouble in any form of the game. For money, White takes full value from his take but here he gets nothing as he will redouble immediately and play this as Double Match Point.

For money this is a very easy take but at this score White should drop and play from 2-5 down. Red gets full value from his four points and one should always beware of cubes that get the opponent either to the match or to Crawford.

I think that most players would get this one wrong and erroneously take, not recognising the cube has no value.
