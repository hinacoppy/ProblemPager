For money this is a very clear no double as the cube has value to White. At this match score that is not the case as the 8-cube will decide the match.

White’s take point is 26% and he easily has that here but the position is highly volatile as any 5 or 6 by Red followed by a non-double from White loses Red’s market by a country mile.

Not doubling here is a bad blunder (0.13). Some would fear putting the match on the line but put yourself in White’s shoes – do you really want to see that cube coming over on 8.
