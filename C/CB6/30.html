Money Play. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=117 White=77
At first sight this looks like a monster double but it is not so in real life.

Red has seven misses (on all of which White will redouble to 16) and if he hits with a 1 his 8-pt is likely to disappear. Meanwhile White is 40 pips up in the race with the stronger home board. In fact it is the take that is trivial and the double that is in question.

A rollout has either double or no double as correct so take your pick! In practice most players would redouble this because a fair few Whites will pass, seeing only the downside of the position.
