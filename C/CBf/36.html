Match Play. Red trails 0-2 to 7. Should Red redouble? If redoubled, should White take?
pips Red=129 White=102
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
A redouble in this position for money would be horrendous and would correctly be met by a swift beaver.

The match score changes everything. A redoubled gammon gives Red the match and now it is a blunder not to redouble. If the cube was still in the centre then the position would not be a double.

Once you think about it logically the redouble becomes clear but a high percentage of players would miss this redouble simply because they would never consider it because they would think about the position in money-game mode.

Our understanding of match play doubling is really still in its infancy but positions like this help us to build our knowledge base.
