As per the comment this is one that I have ‘learnt’ from XG.

Correct by a long way is 6/4(2), 3/1(2)*. This takes away a tempo from White and follows the concept of attacking a single rear checker.

Other moves perhaps look prettier but don’t play to the demands of the position. This sort of position recurs often enough that it should be in everyone’s knowledge
