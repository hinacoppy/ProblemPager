Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=98 White=101
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
A volatile position but is it good enough to double? Red will lose his market with a 4 or a 5 followed by a fan but he also has some bad numbers, 62 for example.

According to the rollouts there is no difference in equity between doubling and not doubling. Of course if you think your opponent is so timid as to drop here then double in a flash but few players would drop this and it would be an octuple blunder to do so!

In practice I held the cube here, hit and doubled my opponent out on the next roll.
