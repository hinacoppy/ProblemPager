Money Play. How should Red play 11?
pips Red=99 White=99
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
This one is fairly straightforward.

The race is close so Red will to block White’s 6s. That makes 13/11(2) mandatory. Note that Red has plenty of spares to play with while White has only a couple of spare 6s. He may be forced off Red’s 5-pt at a time he doesn’t really want to run.

Any play other 13/11(2) is very nearly a blunder.
