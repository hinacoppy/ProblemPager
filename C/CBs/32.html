Any hitting play is at least a double blunder and gives White defensive chances.

The best play 18/16(2), 6/4(2), running when ahead and creating a new home board point. Second best is 7/5(2), 6/4(2).

The key is to recognise that hitting here is completely wrong. After 18/16(2), 6/4(2) Red will not be far from a double.
