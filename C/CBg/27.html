Money Play. How should Red play 22?
pips Red=161 White=158
Red has a lead in development and he shouldn’t passively make two new points, attractive though that may look.

13/7* is virtually mandatory here. As well as gaining a tempo it also develops that ugly stack of checkers on the mid-point.

The question is how to play the 2? 7/5 is too bold and 6/4 is the wrong place for that checker so it is a choice between 24/22 and 13/11. The latter follows Magriel’s ‘hit and split’ maxim while 13/11 continues to develop the extra checkers on the mid-point.

The rollouts have 13/11 by a short head but it’s very close and 24/22 is certainly not an error.
