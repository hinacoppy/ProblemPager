pips Red=126 White=140
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
There are two reasonable plans:

a) Positional: play 13/10/(2), 8/5(2) which leaves Red well ahead in the race and with a reasonably flexible position.
b) Blitz: play 8/5(2), 4/1(2)*

Given the match score and the impact of gammons I would have voted for b) but it turns out the two plans generate nearly identical equities. In fact, Red wins nearly as many gammons by adopting a) rather than b).
