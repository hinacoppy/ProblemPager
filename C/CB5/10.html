Money Play. How should Red play 11?
pips Red=163 White=145
With White having the better board Red cannot play loosely and leave blots lying around.

Clearly 6/5(2) will be two of the ones. The choice then is between the offensive 11/10(2) and the defensive 24/23(2). The two plays are close and it may not be obvious that the key difference between the two plays is in gammon losses Anchored on the 23-pt Red will lose far fewer gammons than remaining on the 24-pt.
