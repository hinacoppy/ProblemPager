I got this wrong over the board by moving 13/7. This is safe for one roll but leaves a structure that may well leave repeating shots over the next few rolls.

It is much better to clear the bar-point with 7/5, 7/3 (a point cleared is a point not to be feared) and hope to clear the blot on the mid-point next time if not hits (only 7 shots).

This position is a good example of taking a small risk now to accrue a gain later. 13/7 is a bad error but not a blunder.
