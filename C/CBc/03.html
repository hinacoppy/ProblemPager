Match Play. Red leads 3-0 to 5. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=51 White=55
Beware the cube that gives your opponent the match.

If White takes he will redouble but winning only gets him to Crawford. That empty 4-pt is ruinous and will cost White dearly.

For money this is an easy take because the cube is live and can be used to good effect by White. After the redouble at this match score the cube is effectively dead and cannot be used to win the game. Taking here is an error while not doubling is a double blunder.
