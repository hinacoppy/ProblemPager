Money Play. How should Red play 43?
pips Red=171 White=149
(CorrectAnswer= 0/0, ErrorScore= 0)
The key to this position is the blot on White's 11-pt. Without that to aim at Red will struggle to win going forwards - he would then have to settle for 7/3, 6/3. However, the blot gives him that possibility.

The correct move is 23/20 (so he can hit the blot on the 14-pt without giving up the 20-pt), 6/2*. Depending upon White's roll he can then decide whether to go forwards or backwards.

12/9, 6/2*, going for the full-blooded blitz is also perfectly reasonable.
