Money Play. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=96 White=128
A classic middle game double. It would be a huge mistake not to double this because there are so many market losers.

Equally well, White has a very easy take. Red has 21 numbers that don’t safety his blot. Red will hit approximately 7 times in those 21 games and will then win a lot of those games in which hits. Add to this White’s racing chances and also his hitting chances as Red clears his 8-, 7- and 6-pts and the take becomes very clear.

Not doubling is a triple blunder while dropping is merely a double blunder!
