Match Play. 4-4 to 7. Should Red double? If doubled. Should White take?
pips Red=113 White=145
White’s broken four-point prime does just enough to give him a take here. Red’s even numbers leave him in trouble and even if he escapes one checker there is no guarantee he will escape the other two.

Naturally Red must double what is a very volatile position but a drop by White is very nearly a blunder. In practical terms I would expect to get a lot of drops here if I doubled as Red.
