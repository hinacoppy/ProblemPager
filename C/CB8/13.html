When in doubt hit. However, in this position there is a lot of doubt.

White’s board is better than Red’s. Red already leads in the race. Playing 24/20, 22/20 protects the lead and guards against anything bad happening to Red.

Hitting gains in the race but that lead may not last that long if Red’s blots are hit and 22/16* gives White a lot of attacking opportunity while disconnecting Red’s rear checkers.

22/16* is so wrong it is a quadruple blunder!!
