Match Play. Red trails 1-3 to 5. Should Red double? If doubled, should White take?
pips Red=148 White=148
For money this is very clearly no double/take but at this match score, 4-away/2-away virtually any sniff of a gammon is enough to turn the cube - the cube is then dead and White’s gammons have no effect.

In fact so strong is Red’s double that White should drop – taking is a double blunder. White needs an anchor to take and at the moment he hasn’t got one and importantly, if things go well for Red, White could find himself closed out.

It is remarkable how little threat Red needs at this score to double. I have been testing this theory in online play and am very pleasantly surprised with the results of being extremely aggressive at this core.
